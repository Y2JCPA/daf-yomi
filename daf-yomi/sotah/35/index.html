Going & Coming — Were the Spies Evil from the Start?

R' Shimon ben Yoḥai
The verse says 'And they went and they came' — likening their going to their coming.
R' Shimon ben Yoḥai
Just as their coming back was with wicked counsel, so too their going was already with wicked intent.
R' Yochanan / R' Meir
Why did the spies praise the land first, then slander it? Because any slander that doesn't begin with truth will not stand.
R' Yochanan / R' Meir
This is represented by the mnemonic: Truth, Alone, Borrowing — three principles of how slander operates.
Insight The spies' strategy was deliberate: establish credibility with praise ('milk and honey') before pivoting to fear ('the people are fierce').

The Spies' Three Distortions

What God Intended

  • 'Stronger than us' (מִמֶּנּוּ) — the Canaanites are strong relative to Israel
  • Deaths in Canaan = God's protection, distracting locals from noticing the spies
  • The spies appeared small to the Canaanites — natural for strangers hiding in trees

How the Spies Twisted It

  • 'Stronger than HIM' (מִמֶּנּוּ) — even God cannot defeat them (heresy!)
  • 'A land that consumes its inhabitants' — the land itself is deadly and cursed
  • 'We were like grasshoppers in their eyes' — Rav Mesharshiyya: How could they know? Liars!
R' Ḥanina bar Pappa The spies spoke heresy: 'Even the Homeowner cannot remove His belongings from there.' They claimed God Himself was powerless against the Canaanites.

The Origin of Tisha B'Av

וַתִּשָּׂא כָּל הָעֵדָה וַיִּתְּנוּ אֶת קוֹלָם וַיִּבְכּוּ
Rabba says in the name of R' Yochanan That night was the eve of the Ninth of Av. The Holy One said: 'On this day they wept a gratuitous weeping — so I will establish it as a day of weeping for future generations.'

The people then tried to stone Joshua and Caleb — R' Ḥiyya bar Abba says they threw stones upward, as if to throw them at God Himself.

The Death of the Spies

SageDescription of Their Punishment
Reish LakishThey died an unusual death — not a natural passing
R' Ḥanina bar Pappa / R' SheilaTheir tongues stretched out to their navels; worms crawled between tongue and navel — measure for measure for their evil speech
Rav Naḥman bar YitzḥakThey died of diphtheria (askara) — choking to death
Measure for Measure The tongue — the instrument of their slander — became the instrument of their punishment. The worms entering and exiting symbolize the destructive cycle of evil speech.
