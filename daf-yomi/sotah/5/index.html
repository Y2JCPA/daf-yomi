What Is the Source Prohibiting Arrogance?

Rava (quoting Ze'eiri)
From Jeremiah 13:15 — "Hear and give ear, be not proud, for the Lord has spoken."
Rav Nachman bar Yitzchak
From Deuteronomy 8:14 — "Lest your heart be lifted up and you forget the Lord." Combined with 8:11: "Beware lest you forget."
R' Avin / R' Ile'a
This works because wherever the Torah says "beware" (הִשָּׁמֶר), "lest" (פֶּן), or "not" (אַל), it indicates a prohibition (לאו).
Principle Arrogance is not merely bad character — it has the status of a biblical prohibition.

The Fate of the Arrogant (Job 24:24)

🧍 Person with arrogance (גַּסּוּת הָרוּחַ)
↓
"They are exalted for a little while"
Raised up only briefly
↓
Does NOT repent
"And they are gone" — dies before his time
"Withers like tops of husks"
REPENTS
"Gathered in as all (כַּכֹּל) others" — dies at proper time, like the Avot who were each blessed with "kol"
Rav Avira Abraham was blessed "bakkol" (Gen 24:1), Isaac "mikkol" (Gen 27:33), Jacob "kol" (Gen 33:11) — the repentant person merits a death like theirs.

God Chooses the Humble

Mountains — God chose Sinai

  • God passed over all tall mountains and hills
  • Rested the Shekhinah on lowly Mount Sinai
  • Did not raise Sinai up — He came DOWN to it
  • Sinai symbolizes humility through its low stature

Trees — God chose the Bush

  • God passed over all beautiful, grand trees
  • Appeared to Moshe in a lowly thornbush (סְנֶה)
  • Rav Yosef: Learn proper conduct from your Creator
  • Humility is the prerequisite for receiving revelation
Rav Huna vs. Rav Chisda One says God elevates the humble to be "with Me"; the other says God descends to be with the humble. The Gemara favors the second — as proven by Sinai.

TeachingSourceSpeaker

The Sotah Warning Procedure (Kinui & Stirah)

👨 Husband warns wife (kinui): "Do not seclude yourself with so-and-so"
↓
👥 Warning must be issued before two witnesses
↓
🚪 If she then secludes herself (stirah) with that man for enough time for defilement
↓
🚫 She becomes forbidden to her husband and forbidden to eat terumah (if a kohen's wife)
↓
💧 She must undergo the sotah water ritual to be permitted again
Abaye's Clarification Warning about 'speaking' + she merely spoke = nothing. Warning about 'seclusion' + she merely spoke = nothing. Only warning about SECLUSION + actual SECLUSION = forbidden.
