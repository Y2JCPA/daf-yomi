How High Did the Jordan's Waters Rise?

R' Yehuda
The water rose 12 mil by 12 mil, parallel to the size of the camp of Israel.
R' Elazar b"R Shimon
Does a person move faster or does water? Water is faster! If the water was only 12 mil high, it would have come crashing down and drowned them before they finished crossing.
R' Elazar b"R Shimon
Rather, the water gathered in heaps upon heaps to over 300 mil high — until all the kings of the East and West saw it and their hearts melted.
R' Ami / R' Yitzchak Nappaha
One says: R' Yehuda holds they crossed in camp formation (all at once); R' Elazar holds they crossed single-file. Another says: both agree they crossed in formation — they argue about whether person or water moves faster.
PROOF TEXT Joshua 5:1 — 'All the kings of the Amorites… heard that Hashem dried up the Jordan… their heart melted.' Even Rahab said: 'We heard how Hashem dried up the Red Sea' (Joshua 2:10).

Joshua's Three Commands While Still in the Jordan

⚔️ Drive out the inhabitants — 'If you do so, good; if not, the water will drown oti and etkhem (me and you)'
↓
🪨 Pick up stones — Each man takes a stone on his shoulder, one per tribe (Joshua 4:5)
↓
👶 A sign for future generations — 'When your children ask: What are these stones?' (Joshua 4:6)
↓
🏨 Lay them at the lodging place — Only that first night's lodging, not every stop along the way
WORDPLAY אותיכם (otikhem) = אותי + אתכם — 'me and you.' Joshua bound his fate to the people's obedience.

Calculating the Weight of the Spies' Cluster of Grapes

FactorCalculation
Weight of each Jordan stone~40 se'a (measured by Abba Ḥalafta, R' Eliezer b. Matya, Ḥananya b. Ḥakhinai)
Shoulder-load principleA person lifts onto his own shoulders ⅓ of what others can load on him
Max carry per person40 × 3 = 120 se'a
'Between two' (Numbers 13:23)Not 2 people on 1 pole, but 2 poles = 4 carriers
R' Yitzchak's reading4 poles like scale-rods — 8 carriers
Total cluster weight8 × 120 ÷ 2 poles = 480 se'a per pole (combined)
Other fruit1 spy carried a pomegranate, 1 carried a fig
Joshua & CalebCarried nothing — either due to dignity or they refused to participate in the plot
KEY INSIGHT The massive weight of the cluster was meant by the spies to frighten the people — 'Look how unnaturally large the produce is!' Joshua and Caleb refused to participate.

God's Command or Moses' Decision? Sending the Spies

📖 "שלח לך" — 'Send for yourself' (Numbers 13:2)
↓
What does לך (for yourself) mean?
Reish Lakish
At your own discretion — not a divine command. God would never choose a bad portion for Himself.
Proof
Moses retold: 'It was good in my eyes' (Deut. 1:23) — in my eyes, not God's.
↓
🔍 R' Ḥiyya bar Abba: The people's intent was to shame (yaḥperu) Eretz Yisrael — same root as ḥafera (embarrassment, Isaiah 24:23)
REISH LAKISH The spies' mission was never commanded by God — it was Moses' concession to the people's lack of faith.

The Spies' Names Reveal Their Character

Sethur ben Michael

  • Sethur — from סתר (to hide): He hid God's miraculous actions
  • Michael — from מך (weak): He made God appear weak, claiming the land couldn't sustain them

Nahbi ben Vophsi

  • Nahbi — from החבי (to conceal): He concealed God's word that the land was good
  • Vophsi — from פסע (to stomp): He stomped on God's attributes, denying His promise
R' YITZCHAK We have a tradition: the spies were named after their deeds. We only preserved the interpretation of these two names.
