Elisha's Bears: One Miracle or Two?

Rav / Shmuel (Opinion 1)
There was one miracle — a forest already existed, but bears appeared miraculously
Rav / Shmuel (Opinion 2)
A miracle within a miracle — neither the forest nor the bears existed beforehand
Gemara
Why was a forest needed at all? Just send bears without a forest!
Gemara (Answer)
Bears are frightened in open areas — they only attack in their natural habitat, the forest
KEY POINT Even miraculous punishment follows natural patterns — bears needed a forest to feel emboldened

Were these offerings rewarded or punished?

Ruth descended from Balak

Three Graces (חן) — R' Yoḥanan

Place → Inhabitants

  • People are fond of their hometown
  • Despite bad water & miscarriages
  • The men of Jericho called it 'pleasant'
  • Source: II Kings 2:19

Wife → Husband

  • A wife finds grace in her husband's eyes
  • Despite her faults
  • He views her positively

Purchase → Buyer

  • A purchased item finds grace with its buyer
  • One views their acquisition favorably
  • Positive bias after commitment

The Golden Rule of Rebuke

שמאל דוחה וימין מקרבת
BARAITA לעולם תהא שמאל דוחה וימין מקרבת

Always let the left hand push away and the right hand draw near.

Two who violated this:
• Elisha — pushed Gehazi away with both hands
• Yehoshua ben Peraḥya — pushed away a student with both hands

Even when rebuking, leave a path for return.
