Is Intimation (Yad) Effective for Charity?

A person says: 'This dinar is for charity, and this…' (without saying 'also')
↓
Is the second coin also designated as charity?
Yes — Intimation Works
Charity is juxtaposed to offerings (Deut. 23:24) — just as intimation works for offerings, it works for charity
No — Intimation Fails
The juxtaposition applies only to 'you shall not delay' — not to other halakhot like intimation
UNRESOLVED The Gemara leaves this dilemma as a teiku — the question of whether intimation applies to charity remains undecided.

What Does 'I Am Ostracized From You' Mean?

Rabbi Akiva
The phrase 'menudna' (ostracized) is a language of distancing — it functions as a vow and one is prohibited from benefiting.
Rabbis (Tanna Kamma)
'Menudna' is a language of excommunication — it is not the terminology people use for vows, so it has no vow-effect.
Abaye
Even Rabbi Akiva concedes there is no flogging for violating this — otherwise the Mishna would say he rules 'stringently,' not that he 'was inclined to be stringent.'
Rav Pappa
The dispute is only about 'menudna.' Everyone agrees 'nadeina' (distanced) = prohibited, and 'meshamattena' (excommunicated) = permitted.
KEY DISTINCTION Rav Pappa narrows the dispute to the ambiguous middle term 'menudna' — is it closer to 'neder' (vow) or 'niddui' (excommunication)?
6 / 9

Rav Pappa vs. Rav Ḥisda on the Scope of the Dispute

Rav Pappa

  • Nadeina (distanced) → All agree: PROHIBITED
  • Menudna (ostracized) → DISPUTED between R' Akiva and Rabbis
  • Meshamattena (excommunicated) → All agree: PERMITTED
  • Dispute is about ONE specific term only

Rav Ḥisda

  • A man said 'I am excommunicated from so-and-so's property'
  • Rav Ḥisda ruled: 'No one is concerned for R' Akiva's opinion'
  • Implies the dispute includes even 'meshamattena'
  • Broader question: Can excommunication language ever express vows?
PRACTICAL IMPACT Rav Ḥisda rules leniently even for explicit excommunication language — disagreeing with Rav Pappa's narrower framing of the dispute.

Minimum waiting time before dissolvingNo minimum — can dissolve immediatelyRav Huna's incident
