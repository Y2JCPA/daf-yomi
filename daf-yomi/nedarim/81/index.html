Nedarim 81 — Grime, Torah & Vow Categories

Shmuel's Teaching: Grime endangers health — head → blindness, clothes → madness, body → boils
↓
Message from Eretz Yisrael: Guard hygiene, study in groups, educate sons of the poor
↓
Why did Eretz Yisrael perish? They did not recite birkat ha-Torah
↓
Vow Categories: Which vows can a husband nullify — affliction vs. matters between him and her?
↓
Rabbanan
Bathing/adorning vows = affliction (innui nefesh)
R' Yosei
Not affliction, but may be matters between him and her
Key Theme This daf weaves together physical well-being, Torah transmission, and the legal categories that determine a husband's power to annul his wife's vows.

Three Messages from Eretz Yisrael to Babylonia

🧼 Be careful with grime — it leads to disease and sickness
↓
👥 Study Torah in groups (b'chaburah), not alone
↓
📖 Educate the sons of paupers — from them Torah will emerge
Prooftext "Water shall flow from his branches (midalyav)" (Numbers 24:7) — expounded as midalim, the poor among him. Torah, compared to water, flows from the impoverished.

Why Don't Torah Scholars Produce Torah Scholar Sons?

Rav Yosef
So people should not say the Torah is their inheritance (yerushshah)
Rav Sheshet b. Rav Idi
So they should not be presumptuous (yitgadderu) toward the community
Mar Zutra
Because they lord over the community using their fathers' standing
Rav Ashi
Because they call ordinary people donkeys (chamorei)
Ravina
Because they do not first recite a blessing over the Torah (birkat ha-Torah)
Ravina's Source Jeremiah 9:11-12 — The Sages and prophets could not explain why Eretz Yisrael was destroyed. Hashem Himself revealed: "Because they forsook My Torah" — meaning they did not recite birkat ha-Torah.

Isi bar Yehuda & Vardimus — Source for Laundry Priority

Isi bar Yehuda stays away from R' Yosei's academy for 3 days — cannot understand R' Yosei's reasoning
↓
Vardimus (R' Yosei's son) asks: What is the difficulty?
↓
R' Yosei says: Their laundry takes precedence over lives of others — what is the source?
↓
Vardimus answers from Numbers 35:3: "for their beasts (chayyatam)"
↓
Not literal beasts
Beasts are already included in 'animals' (behemtam)
Chayyatam = Chiyyuta
Means their lives — laundering is vital for well-being, takes precedence
Bottom Line The verse equates laundering with life-sustaining needs, proving R' Yosei's ruling that residents' laundry takes precedence over outsiders' lives.

Categories of Vows a Husband Can Nullify

Vows of Affliction (Innui Nefesh)

  • Produce is konam for me (food prohibition)
  • Applies whether between him & her OR between her & others
  • Husband can always nullify these

Matters Between Him & Her (Bein Ish l'Ishto)

  • I will not paint my eyes / rouge my cheeks
  • I will not engage in intercourse (konam for me)
  • Only nullifiable if they affect the spousal relationship

Cannot Nullify

  • I won't prepare food for your father/brother
  • I won't place straw before your animal
  • Does not affect her or her spousal relationship
Key Dispute Rabbanan: Bathing/adorning vows = affliction. R' Yosei: Not affliction, but per Rav Adda bar Ahava, R' Yosei agrees they are matters between him and her.
