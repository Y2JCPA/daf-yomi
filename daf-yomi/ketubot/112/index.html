Ketubot 112: The Miraculous Land

אֶרֶץ פְּרִי לִמְלֵחָה מֵרָעַת יוֹשְׁבֵי בָהּ — Psalms 107:34
CORE THEME This daf celebrates the extraordinary fertility of Eretz Yisrael through vivid stories of giant fruit and astonishing agricultural yields, while warning that sin diminishes the land's blessing. It concludes with the Sages' deep physical love for the land and prophecies of the messianic era.

🍑 The Giant Peach of Kefar Hino

🍑 R' Ḥelbo, R' Avira & R' Yosei bar Ḥanina visit a certain place
↓
🫕 Locals bring a peach the size of a stewpot (ilpas) — capacity of 5 se'a
↓
🍽️ They ate ⅓, declared ⅓ ownerless, and fed ⅓ to their animals
↓
📅 Next year: R' Elazar visits — the peach fits in one hand
↓
📖 He quotes Psalms 107:34: "A fruitful land into a salt waste, from the wickedness of they who dwell there"
PRINCIPLE The fertility of the land is directly tied to the spiritual merit of its inhabitants — sin causes the produce to diminish.

🍇 The Grape Clusters of Gavla

R' Yehoshua ben Levi
Calves are standing between the grapevines! Aren't you concerned about damage?
Locals
Those are not calves — they are grape clusters!
R' Yehoshua ben Levi
O earth, gather in your fruit! For whom do you produce? For the gentiles who stand over us in our sins?
R' Ḥiyya (next year)
Goats are standing between the vines! (Clusters had shrunk from calf-size to goat-size)
Locals
Go away! Don't do to us what your colleague already did!
RESULT R' Yehoshua ben Levi's curse was fulfilled — the fruit shrank because the land's bounty was benefiting foreign rulers rather than Israel.

🌾 Fertility Comparison: Eretz Yisrael vs. Egypt

LocationQualityYield per Beit Se'a
Zoan, EgyptBest land in the best country outside Israel70 kor
Beit She'an valleyGood irrigated land outside Israel's borders70 kor (R' Meir)
Hebron (rocky terrain)Rockiest land in Eretz Yisrael — burial ground490 kor (7× Zoan)
Non-rocky Eretz YisraelRegular arable land of IsraelUp to 500 kor
Blessed year (Isaac)Hundredfold blessing (Genesis 26:12)50,000 kor
KEY PROOF Nivneta (Numbers 13:22) — "Hebron was built seven years before Zoan" means it was seven times more fertile, not literally built earlier. A father wouldn't build for his younger son (Canaan) before his older son (Mizraim).

📐 Deriving the 50,000 Kor Calculation

Zoan (best of Egypt) produces 70 kor per beit se'a
↓
Hebron = 7× Zoan = 490 kor (from Numbers 13:22)
↓
Non-rocky Israel yields up to ~500 kor
↓
Isaac's blessed year = 100× normal (Genesis 26:12)
↓
500 × 100 = 50,000 kor per beit se'a in a blessed year
R' YOSEI ADDS One se'a of Judean wheat yields 5 se'a: flour, fine flour, bran fiber, coarse bran, and kiburaya (inferior flour).

🦌 Why Is Eretz Yisrael Called a "Deer" (Tzvi)?

The Deer's Skin

  • Once a deer is skinned, its hide shrinks and cannot contain its meat
  • The skin appears small relative to the animal's full body

Eretz Yisrael

  • When inhabited by Israel, the land expands to hold everyone
  • When Israel is exiled, the land contracts and appears small
  • Source: Jeremiah 3:19 — "a pleasant land, the goodliest (tzvi) heritage"
INSIGHT The land's capacity is not fixed — it miraculously adjusts to match the spiritual state of the Jewish people.
