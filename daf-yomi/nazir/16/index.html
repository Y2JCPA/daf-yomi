Impurity on the Last Day: Who Negates What?

Tanna Kamma

  • Unspecified nazirite impure on day 30 → negates entire tally
  • Explicit 30-day vow impure on day 30 → negates entire tally
  • 100-day vow impure on day 100 → negates entire tally
  • 100-day vow impure on day 101 → negates only 30 days

R' Eliezer

  • Unspecified nazirite impure on day 30 → negates only 7 days
  • Explicit 30-day vow impure on day 30 → agrees: negates entire tally
  • 100-day vow impure on day 100 → negates only 30 days
  • 100-day vow impure on day 101 → negates only 7 days
R' Eliezer's Logic For an unspecified vow, day 30 is after completion of the term, so impurity at that point only negates 7 days — the purification period.

Does Naziriteship Take Effect in a Cemetery?

R' Yochanan
Naziriteship takes effect — it is pending (taluy) from the moment he vows, and activates when he becomes pure.
Reish Lakish
Naziriteship does NOT take effect at all. If he doesn't repeat his vow after leaving, he is not a nazirite.
R' Yochanan
Proof from the Mishna: it says he doesn't bring impurity offerings — implying the naziriteship does take effect, just without the offering obligation!
Reish Lakish
No — the Mishna means he is not included in the law of impurity at all, because he is simply not a nazirite.
R' Yochanan
But a baraita teaches: an impure person who vows naziriteship is prohibited from shaving, wine, and impurity — and receives 40 lashes for violations!
R' Yochanan
If naziriteship doesn't take effect while impure, why would he receive lashes?
Key Objection The baraita (Tosefta 2:14) states that an impure nazirite receives malkot (lashes) for violations — strong evidence that the vow takes effect even in a state of impurity.

↓
📜 Day 31 — Second term begins immediately after shaving
↓
⏳ Days 31–60 — Observes second nazirite term
↓
✂️ Day 61 — Shaves for second term (day 60 valid; even day 59 valid per R' Pappeyas)
Overlap Principle The 30th day of the first term can count as the first day of the second term — this is why shaving on day 59 from the start can fulfill the second term.
