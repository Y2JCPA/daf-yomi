How Long Is a Standard Naziriteship?

Rav Mattana
An unspecified naziriteship is 30 days. The thirtieth day can count toward both terms via miktzat hayom k'kulo.
Bar Padda
An unspecified naziriteship is 29 days. The mishna's case of shaving on day 30 for the first term proves this.
Gemara
Each side claims support from different clauses of the mishna. The Gemara tests both positions against multiple mishnayot.
Core Dispute The debate hinges on whether the 30th day is the last day of naziriteship (Rav Mattana) or the first day after naziriteship ends on day 29 (Bar Padda).

Rav Mattana vs Bar Padda: Duration of Naziriteship

Rav Mattana

  • Unspecified naziriteship = 30 days
  • Shaving on day 31 is the standard case
  • Shaving on day 30 uses miktzat hayom k'kulo
  • Day 30 can count for BOTH terms of consecutive naziriteships

Bar Padda

  • Unspecified naziriteship = 29 days
  • Shaving on day 30 is the standard case
  • Mishna clause about shaving on day 31 = one who specified 30 complete days
  • Proof from: shaving for 2nd term on day 60 (= 2×29 + 2 shaving days)
Key Difference They each claim a different clause of the mishna as the straightforward reading and reinterpret the other clause.

Impurity on the 30th Day: Who Has the Problem?

Mishna
If an unspecified nazirite becomes impure on day 30, it negates the entire tally and he must restart.
Rav Mattana
This works well — day 30 is still within his naziriteship, so impurity negates it.
Bar Padda
Difficulty! If naziriteship ended on day 29, why does day 30 impurity negate anything?
Bar Padda
Answer: Look at the latter clause — R' Eliezer says impurity negates only 7 days. If naziriteship were 30 days, he should negate all of them! This proves it's 29.
Challenge Pattern The Gemara repeatedly tests each position against different mishnayot, forcing each sage to reinterpret the clause that challenges them.

Tannaitic Source: Rabbi Yoshiya vs Rabbi Yonatan

R' Yoshiya
"Days" implies minimum of 2 days. But the verse adds "he shall let his hair grow long" — significant hair growth requires 30 days.
R' Yonatan
"Until the days are complete" — what days need completion? The days of a month. A month can be 29 or 30 days.
Gemara
Perhaps Rav Mattana follows R' Yoshiya (30 days from hair growth) and Bar Padda follows R' Yonatan (month = possibly 29)?
Resolution Attempt Rav Mattana responds: Both may agree naziriteship is 30 days. They disagree about whether "until" (ad) means until but not including or until and including the 30th day.
