Nazir 57 — Uncertain Nazirites & the Prohibition of Rounding

DAF OVERVIEW The Gemara resolves which element of nazirite impurity is a halakha l'Moshe miSinai versus a kal vachomer. A mishna about two nazirites with uncertain impurity raises questions about domain classification and the prohibition of hakafat ha-rosh (rounding the head). The daf features a dramatic exchange between Rav Huna and Rav Adda bar Ahava about whether women may shave the heads of minor boys.
3 / 9

Which Rule Is the Halakha L'Moshe MiSinai?

Dilemma
Is the barley-grain-bulk bone the halakha l'Moshe miSinai, and R' Akiva tried to derive the quarter-log of blood via kal vachomer?
Alternative
Or is the quarter-log of blood the halakha l'Moshe miSinai, and R' Akiva used the bone as the source for the kal vachomer?
Baraita
A bone of barley-grain-bulk is the halakha. A quarter-log of blood is the kal vachomer. And one does not derive a kal vachomer from a halakha.
PRINCIPLE אין דנין קל וחומר מהלכה — One cannot derive a kal vachomer inference from a halakha l'Moshe miSinai.

Two Uncertain Nazirites — The Mishna's Procedure

👁️ A witness says: "I saw one of you become impure, but I don't know which"
↓
📅 Both nazirites must complete their current naziriteship terms
↓
✂️ Both shave their hair
↓
🐑 Together they bring one offering of impurity and one of purity
↓
🗣️ Each says: "If I am impure, the impurity-offering is mine and the purity-offering is yours"
↓
📅 They each count a further 30 days of naziriteship
↓
🐑 Together they bring another offering of purity, with each assigning the earlier and later offerings accordingly
KEY POINT By conditionally assigning each offering, both nazirites fulfill their obligations regardless of which one was actually impure.

Private vs. Public Domain — Why Are They Impure?

Uncertain impurity with 3 people present (2 nazirites + witness)
↓
Rule from sotah: uncertain impurity in a private domain = impure
↓
But sotah involves only 2 people! With 3, it should be a public domain = pure!
↓
Rabba bar Rav Huna
The witness saw from a distance — he was not present, so only 2 people were there
Rav Ashi
The mishna's language — "I do not know which" — confirms the witness was far away
DOMAIN RULE Uncertain impurity: private domain (≤2 people) = treated as impure; public domain (≥3 people) = treated as pure.

The Rounding Prohibition — Why Can They Shave?

Gemara
Why may they shave? Perhaps one is not impure — his shaving would violate hakafat ha-rosh (rounding the corners of the head)!
Shmuel
The mishna refers to women or minors, who are not subject to the prohibition of rounding the head.
Gemara
Why not say it refers to an adult male, and rounding the entire head is not called rounding?
Inference
Since Shmuel doesn't say this, we conclude: Shmuel holds that rounding the entire head IS called rounding.
HALAKHIC INFERENCE הקפת כל הראש שמה הקפה — Shaving the entire head is included in the prohibition of rounding, according to Shmuel.

Rav Huna vs. Rav Adda bar Ahava — Rounding a Minor's Head

Rav Huna

  • An adult who rounds a minor's head is liable for lashes
  • Verse links rounding and destroying the beard: whoever has the beard prohibition has the rounding prohibition
  • Women — no beard prohibition → no rounding prohibition → may shave a child's head
  • His wife Ḥova shaved their sons' hair

Rav Adda bar Ahava

  • An adult who rounds a minor's head is not liable
  • "You shall not round" (plural) links the one who rounds to the one who is rounded
  • Since the minor is not liable → the adult who rounds him is also not liable
  • Cursed Ḥova for shaving the children — tragically, Rav Huna's children died during Rav Adda's lifetime
CORE DISPUTE Both agree rounding the entire head is prohibited. They disagree on whether an adult can be liable for rounding the head of a minor.
