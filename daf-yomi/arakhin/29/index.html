📜 Moses Ascends to Heaven

Moses ascends on High and finds God tying crowns (taggin/זיונין) on the letters of the Torah
↓
Moses: "Master of the Universe — who prevents You from giving the Torah without these crowns?"
↓
God: "A man named Akiva ben Yosef will one day derive heaps upon heaps of laws from each stroke"
↓
Moses goes and sits in the eighth row of Akiva's study hall — and cannot follow a word of the discussion. His strength wanes.
↓
Students challenge Akiva for a source. Akiva answers: "It is a law given to Moses at Sinai." Moses is comforted.
↓
Moses returns to God: "Why give Torah through me and not Akiva?" God: "Be silent. This intention arose before Me."

🕎 How Tall Was the Menorah?

Rav
The height of the Candelabrum is nine handbreadths.
Rav Shimi bar Ḥiyya
But there was a stone before the Menorah with three steps upon which the priest stood to tend the lamps! Why would steps be needed if the Menorah were only nine handbreadths tall?
Rav
Shimi! Is it you who challenges me so? I did not mean the total height — which is eighteen handbreadths. I meant the Menorah's lamp portion begins nine handbreadths above the base.
Resolution Total Menorah height = 18 handbreadths. The lamps are positioned at the upper 9 handbreadths, hence the need for steps.

🔥 Three Heavenly Vessels Shown to Moses

Ark of Fire

  • An Ark of fire descended from Heaven
  • Moses viewed its heavenly form
  • Fashioned the earthly Ark in its likeness

Table of Fire

  • A Table of fire descended from Heaven
  • Moses viewed its heavenly form
  • Fashioned the earthly Table in its likeness

Candelabrum of Fire

  • A Menorah of fire descended from Heaven
  • Menorah was among the three things Moses found most difficult to grasp
  • God pointed His finger; the angel Gabriel wore a craftsman's belt to demonstrate the technique

חי במרומי עולם — "He lives in the heights of the universe"
🏛️ A Hump on the Ḥet — God Lives in the Heights The Rabbis placed a hump-like stroke on the roof of the letter ḥet (ח) — as if to say: The Holy One, Blessed be He, lives in the heights of the universe.

They also suspended the left leg of the letter heh (ה), leaving a gap — alluding to repentance: though one may exit easily through the open bottom, returning requires the gap at the top, with Heaven's assistance.

🌍 Why These Letters Created the Worlds?

"When they were created" (בְּהִבָּרְאָם)
Genesis 2:4 — read as בְּהֵ בְּרָאָם: "He created them with the letter ה"
↓
ה — This World
Shaped like a portico — open at the bottom.
Anyone who wishes to sin may easily depart.
Return requires squeezing through the suspended left leg — only with Heaven's help (Reish Lakish)
י — World-to-Come
The smallest letter — because the righteous are very few.
Its bent head alludes to the righteous who lower their heads in shame before the wicked in this world
