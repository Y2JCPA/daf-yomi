☁️ Cosmic Origin of Rain: Ocean Waters or Upper Heavens?

Rabbi Eliezer
Rain originates from the ocean. The verse 'darkness [חֹשֶׁכַת] of waters' (Ps. 18:12) — swapping one letter — yields 'distillation of waters.' Ocean water rises, is sweetened in the clouds, and falls as fresh rain.
Rabbi Yehoshua
Rain comes from the upper waters suspended in the heavens by God's word: 'Who waters the mountains from His upper chambers' (Ps. 104:13). These chambers' 'fruit' is the rainwater that falls to earth.
Rav Dimi
The entire world drinks from the overflow of the Garden of Eden: 'A river went out of Eden to water the garden' (Gen. 2:10). From the runoff of a beit kor, a beit se'ah is irrigated — Eden sustains all.
Gemara
Reconciliation: The upper waters opinion aligns with a baraita: upper waters 'have no defined place' — they are suspended by divine word, and their fruit is rainwater. This is consistent with Rabbi Yehoshua.
Key Textual Move (Amud A §5) חֹשֶׁכַת מָיִם vs. חַשְׁרַת מָיִם — a kaf for a reish. Combine both verses and you get: ocean water sweetened (נִמְתַּק) in the clouds. A single-letter swap unlocks a cosmological teaching.

📅 When Does One Begin Requesting Rain?

Tanna Kamma

  • 3rd of Marḥeshvan
  • 15 days after Shemini Atzeret
  • Allows pilgrims time to reach home (not the Euphrates)
  • Insert: וְתֵן טַל וּמָטָר in blessing of the years

Rabban Gamliel ✅ Halakha

  • 7th of Marḥeshvan
  • Extra 4 days for last pilgrim to reach the Euphrates River
  • Rabbi Elazar rules: halakha follows Rabban Gamliel
  • Standard practice in Eretz Yisrael today

Diaspora — Ḥananya

  • 60 days after Tekufat Tishrei
  • ~December 4 or 5
  • Babylonia has abundant water; less urgency for rain
  • Shmuel's mnemonic: 'when wood is brought into the house of Tavut the bird hunter'

🛤️ Joseph's Four Pieces of Travel Wisdom (Gen. 45:24)

'See that you not fall out by the way' — Do not engage in new halachic debate while traveling (Rabbi Elazar). Preoccupation with legal analysis can cause you to stumble into danger.
↓
Rote review is permitted — The prohibition applies only to new halachic analysis (חידוש), not to reviewing familiar material (שינון). This reconciles Rabbi Elai bar Berekhya's teaching that scholars who don't discuss Torah are 'worthy of being burned.'
↓
Do not take long strides — Large strides diminish a person's eyesight by one five-hundredth. Preserve your sight; enter the city before dark.
↓
Enter the city before sunset — Leave each place saying 'it is good' and enter each new place saying 'it is good' (Rav Yehuda citing Rav). Preserve your favorable impression of every place.
↓
Eat modestly on the road — Eat no more per day than you would in a year of famine (R. Ḥiyya). The body on the road cannot digest as it does at home.

🌍 Eretz Yisrael's Cosmic Priority

אֶרֶץ יִשְׂרָאֵל שׁוֹתָה מֵי גְּשָׁמִים וְכָל הָעוֹלָם כּוּלּוֹ שׁוֹתֶה מִן הַמּוֹתָר
Baraita — The Land Was Created First Eretz Yisrael was created before the rest of the world — 'while as yet He had not made the land, nor the fields' (Prov. 8:26) uses a unique diminutive form. Eretz Yisrael drinks directly from rainwater; the whole world drinks only from the residue left in the clouds. The cosmic scale: Egypt (400×400 parasangs) = 1/60 of Cush = 1/60 of the world = 1/60 of Gan Eden = 1/60 of the Deep (תְּהוֹם).
