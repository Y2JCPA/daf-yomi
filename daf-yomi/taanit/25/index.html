🪙 The Golden Table Leg

R. Ḥanina's Wife
Until when will we suffer this poverty? Pray that something be given to us from Heaven!
Heaven
A golden table leg descended from Heaven in response to his prayer.
R. Ḥanina ben Dosa
She dreamed the righteous eat at complete golden tables in the World-to-Come. Our table will be defective. Pray the leg be taken back!
R. Ḥanina's Wife
What can we do? I will pray for the leg to be returned.
Tradition
The last miracle was greater than the first — it is harder for Heaven to take back than to give.
Principle R. Ḥanina preferred a complete table in the World-to-Come over earthly reward, even rejecting a miraculous heavenly gift.

✨ Three Miracles of R. Ḥanina ben Dosa

🍞 The Oven Full of Bread

  • Wife heated oven to hide poverty from neighbors
  • Evil neighbor came to expose her shame
  • Found the oven full of bread and basin full of dough
  • A miracle was performed for her honor

🕯️ Vinegar Burns Like Oil

  • Daughter confused vinegar for oil on Shabbat eve
  • Ḥanina: 'He who commanded oil to burn can command vinegar'
  • The vinegar lamp burned all day until Havdalah
  • Let the One who commanded oil to burn — command vinegar

🐐 Goats Bring Back Bears

  • Neighbors: 'Your goats damage our fields!'
  • Ḥanina: 'If true, let bears eat them; if not, let each return a bear'
  • Next morning — each goat led home a bear on its horns
  • How did he have goats? Lost chicken → eggs → flock → sold for goats

💭 Rabbi Elazar ben Pedat's Dream Dialogue

Extreme poverty: After bloodletting, R. Elazar had only a clove of garlic to eat — he fainted
↓
In his dream, the Holy One sat with him.
Elazar asked: 'Until when will I suffer such poverty in this world?'
↓
God: 'Do you want Me to destroy the world and re-create it — perhaps you will be born in a time of sustenance?'
↓
Elazar Refuses
'And even then it is only a possibility?'
He declined — the years already lived outnumber those remaining
God's Reward
13 rivers of pure balsam oil in the World-to-Come
as vast as the Euphrates and Tigris
↓
Elazar woke laughing and crying — laughing at the great reward; crying that such reward was for so brief a life

🌊 Ridya: The Angel of Rain

רִדְיָא — מַלְאַךְ הַגֶּשֶׁם עוֹמֵד בֵּין תְּהוֹם עֶלְיוֹן לְתַחְתּוֹן
Mystical Vision of Rabba Rabba said: I saw the angel Ridya — in the form of a calf with parted lips, standing between the lower waters of the deep and the upper waters. To the upper waters he says: 'Distill your water downward.' To the lower waters: 'Rise up.'

Separately, Rabbi Elazar taught: During Sukkot's water libation, the waters of the deep call to one another — 'Let your waters flow, I hear the voices of our friends, the wine and water libations!'
