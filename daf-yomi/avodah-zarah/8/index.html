Where to Insert Personal Requests in Prayer

Shmuel (via Rav Yehuda)
The halakha is: insert personal requests in Shome'a Tefillah (שומע תפילה).
Rav
Although the Sages said Shome'a Tefillah, one may also add requests at the end of each relevant blessing that matches the request's nature.
Rav Ḥiyya bar Ashi (citing Rav)
If he has a sick person at home — pray in the blessing of the sick. Needs sustenance? Pray in Birkat HaShanim.
R' Yehoshua ben Levi
After finishing the Amida, one may add personal supplications even as long as the Yom Kippur confession.
Key Principle The default place for personal requests is Shome'a Tefillah, but the Gemara provides multiple valid alternatives — within relevant blessings or after the Amida.

Mnemonic Kalenda (after solstice) is listed FIRST in the Mishna; Saturnalia (before solstice) is listed AFTER — like the verse: "You have hemmed me in behind and before" (Psalms 139:5), where 'before' appears after 'behind.'

Adam HaRishon & the Shortening Days

🌍 Adam is created and observes the world
↓
🌘 Days grow progressively shorter after the autumnal equinox
↓
😰 Adam fears: "Because I sinned, the world is returning to chaos!"
↓
🙏 He fasts and prays for eight days
↓
☀️ The winter solstice (Tevet) arrives — days begin lengthening
↓
🎉 Adam declares an eight-day festival in gratitude
↓
📅 Next year: he observes both the 8 fast-days AND 8 feast-days
↓
⚠️ Adam established them for Heaven; gentiles later corrupted them for idolatry
Question If the world was created in Nisan (spring), Adam had already seen long days! The Gemara answers: he had never seen days this short — the extreme brevity near the solstice was new and alarming.

Rome & Israel: Alliance to Subjugation

Partnership Phase (26 Years)

  • Rome fought 32 battles against Greece — couldn't win alone
  • Partnered with the Jewish people to finally defeat the Greeks
  • Agreement: kings from one nation, governors from the other
  • Rome used the 'pearl and precious stone' argument to claim Greek submission
  • Verse: "Let us take our journey" (Gen. 33:12) — Esau treats Jacob as equal

Subjugation Phase

  • After 26 years, Rome turned against the Jews
  • 180 years before the Temple's destruction: Rome imposed rule on Israel
  • 80 years before: Sages decreed impurity on gentile lands & glass vessels
  • 40 years before: Sanhedrin exiled from the Chamber of Hewn Stone
  • Verse: "Let my lord pass over before his servant" (Gen. 33:14) — Jacob submits to Esau
Source R' Yishmael son of R' Yosei transmitted these historical statements from his father R' Yosei ben Ḥalafta when he fell ill and the Sages asked him to share teachings they had forgotten.
