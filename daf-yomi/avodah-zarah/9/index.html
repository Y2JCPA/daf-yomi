Rav Pappa: Converting Between Dating Systems

A tanna forgot how many years since the destruction of the Temple
↓
Ask a scribe what year he writes on documents
(Scribes count from the start of Greek rule, 380 years before the destruction)
↓
Tanna needs his year
Add 20 to the scribe's number
Mnemonic: "These twenty years I've been in your house" (Bereishit 31:41)
Scribe needs his year
Subtract 20 from the tanna's number
Mnemonic: A scribe deducts, a tanna adds (tosfa'a)
Wordplay Tanna tosfa'a — a pun meaning both 'the tanna adds on' and 'to teach the Tosefta,' the classic role of a tanna.

The 6,000-Year World Timeline

2,000 Years of Void (תוהו)

  • From Creation to Avraham
  • The Torah had not yet been given
  • The world was spiritually 'waste'

2,000 Years of Torah (תורה)

  • Begins when Avraham was 52 years old
  • Counted from 'the souls they made in Charan'
  • Torah began spreading in the world

2,000 Years of Mashiach (משיח)

  • The final era of history
  • Due to our sins, years have been 'lost'
  • Mashiach has not yet arrived
School of Eliyahu This teaching from the Tanna d'vei Eliyahu is one of the most famous chronological frameworks in the Talmud.

When Do the 2,000 Years of Torah Begin?

Initial Suggestion
Perhaps from Matan Torah (the giving of the Torah at Sinai)?
Gemara
That doesn't work! Creation to Sinai = 2,448 years. That's already 448 years into the third millennium — the Torah era would encroach on the Messianic era.
Gemara's Answer
Rather, it begins from Avraham and Sarah in Charan — 'the souls they had made' (Bereishit 12:5) — when they began spreading Torah.
Gemara
Avraham was 52 years old at that time. From then until Sinai = exactly 448 years — perfectly accounting for the difference.
Rav Pappa's Second Formula To convert between tanna's Messianic count and scribe's document date: add or subtract 48. Mnemonic: 'Forty-eight cities' (Bamidbar 35:7).

Rav Huna b. Rav Yehoshua: Finding Your Shemitah Year

1️⃣ Take the years since the destruction of the Temple
↓
2️⃣ Add 1 year to align with the start of a Sabbatical cycle
↓
3️⃣ Calculate centuries as Jubilee cycles (every 49 years)
↓
4️⃣ Deduct 2 years per century (Jubilee year counts in the next cycle)
↓
5️⃣ Divide the remaining years by 7 — the remainder is your Shemitah position
Mnemonic For the 2-year deduction per century: "For these two years the famine has been in the land" (Bereishit 45:6).
