Is Omitting Acharayut a Scribal Error?

Shmuel
The Rabbis hold that omitting a property guarantee (acharayut) from a promissory note is a scribal error — no one lends money without security.
Rava bar Ittai
But Shmuel himself says a scribe must consult the seller about writing enhancement, superior-quality land, and lien clauses — implying guarantee is not automatic!
Shmuel
No contradiction: for promissory notes, a person doesn't give away money for nothing — guarantee is assumed. For land sales, a buyer may accept the risk — a person is apt to purchase land for a day.
Distinction Promissory notes: guarantee is always assumed (scribal error if omitted). Deeds of sale: guarantee must be explicitly written.

The Story of Avuh bar Ihi

אדם עשוי ליקח קרקע ליום אחד
Case Study Avuh bar Ihi bought a loft from his sister. Her creditor came and repossessed it. Avuh came before Mar Shmuel demanding compensation.

Shmuel asked: Did she write you a guarantee?
Avuh: No.
Shmuel: Then go in peace — nothing can be done.
Avuh: But Master, you said omission of guarantee is a scribal error!
Shmuel: That applies only to promissory notes, not to buying and selling — a person is apt to purchase land for a day.

Abaye: Can Reuven Litigate with the Creditor?

Reuven sells field to Shimon
↓
Reuven's creditor repossesses the field from Shimon
↓
Can Reuven challenge the creditor in court?
↓
With Guarantee
✅ Yes — Reuven says: What you take from Shimon comes back to me, so I am a legal party
Without Guarantee (Yesh Omrim)
✅ Still Yes — Reuven says: I don't want Shimon to have a grievance against me
Abaye's Principle The original seller always has standing to challenge the creditor, whether he sold with or without a guarantee.

Buyer's Right to Withdraw (No Guarantee)

📜 Reuven sells a field to Shimon without guarantee
↓
⚠️ Disputants emerge challenging Reuven's prior ownership
↓
❓ Has Shimon taken possession (walked the borders)?
↓
❌ Before possession: Shimon can withdraw — no payment required
↓
✅ After possession: Cannot withdraw — it was like buying a tied sack (חמרא סתומה) whose contents are unknown
↓
📋 Yesh Omrim: Even with guarantee — seller says: Show me your court authorization document, then I'll pay
Key Moment Possession is established when the buyer walks along the borders of the field to inspect them.

Rav vs. Shmuel: Compensation After Repossession

Rav

  • Buyer receives money paid for field ✅
  • Buyer receives enhancement value ✅
  • Full compensation for buyer's investment

Shmuel

  • Buyer receives money paid for field ✅
  • Buyer does NOT receive enhancement value ❌
  • Even if seller specified enhancement payment in deed
  • Reason: Without land returning to seller, payment looks like ribbit (interest)
Shmuel's Logic Since the seller never gets the land back, the buyer's payment looks like a loan — and paying for enhancement resembles paying interest on that loan.
