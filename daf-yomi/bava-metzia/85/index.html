🔥 Two Great Sufferers: Rabbi Elazar vs. Rabbi Yehuda HaNasi

Rabbi Elazar b. Rabbi Shimon

  • Afflictions came upon him out of love (מאהבה)
  • Left him without a triggering incident — spontaneous healing
  • During his suffering: no one died prematurely in the world
  • His suffering was greater and more meritorious than Rabbi's
  • Atonement extended to protect the entire generation

Rabbi Yehuda HaNasi (Rebbi)

  • Afflictions came due to a specific incident (the calf)
  • Left him due to another specific incident (the weasels)
  • During his suffering: world did not need his prayers for rain
  • Stableman wealthier than King Shapur of Persia
  • Suffering accepted voluntarily after hearing of Rabbi Elazar's example

🦌 Rabbi Ḥiyya's Plan to Preserve the Entire Torah

Step 1: Sow flax seeds in the field
↓
Step 2: Spin and twine nets from the harvested flax
↓
Step 3: Hunt deer using the nets
↓
Step 4: Feed the deer meat to orphaned children
↓
Step 5: Prepare parchment from the deer hides
↓
Step 6: Write the Five Books of Torah on the parchment
↓
Step 7: Travel to towns without teachers — teach five children one book of Torah each; teach six children one order of Mishnah each
↓
Result: "If the Torah were forgotten from Israel, I could restore it entirely" — Rabbi Yehuda HaNasi declared: "How great are the deeds of Ḥiyya!"

⚡ Elijah Delayed at New Moon — The Secret of Rain

Rabbi Yehuda HaNasi
Why were you delayed today? You always come on Rosh Chodesh — what happened?
Elijah
I had to wake Abraham, wash his hands, wait for him to pray, then lay him back to rest. Then Isaac. Then Jacob. I cannot rouse them all at once — if they prayed together they would bring the Messiah before his time.
Rabbi Yehuda HaNasi
Is there anyone alive in this world comparable to the Patriarchs, who can produce such powerful prayers?
Elijah
Yes — Rabbi Ḥiyya and his sons!
Rabbi Yehuda HaNasi
[Decrees a fast. Rabbi Ḥiyya and his sons lead the prayers. When they reach Gevurot — the blessing for rain — rain immediately begins to fall.]
Heaven
Who has revealed secrets in this world? It is Elijah! [Elijah is brought up and beaten with sixty fiery lashes]
Elijah
[Returns to earth disguised as a fiery bear and scatters the congregation so they cannot complete the prayer service]
⚡ Lesson Even the most powerful prayers can be too potent — Heaven guards the precise timing of redemption and punishes the premature revelation of its secrets.
