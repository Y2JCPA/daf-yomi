☠️ When Are Conspiring Witnesses Executed?

Sadducees
Conspiring witnesses are only executed if the accused was already put to death based on their testimony — 'a life for a life' means a life was already taken first.
Rabbis
They are executed only when the verdict is concluded but the accused has NOT yet been killed. The verse says 'as he conspired to do to his brother' — his brother must still be alive!
The Decisive Verse "And you shall do to him as he conspired to do to his brother" (Deut. 19:19) — the word 'brother' implies the accused is still living when the conspirators receive their punishment.

⚖️ Why Aren't Lashes Divided Among Conspiring Witnesses?

Abaye
The word "wicked" (רָשָׁע) appears in the law of lashes and also in the law of conspiring witnesses. This gezera shava (verbal analogy) teaches: just as lashes are given in full to a single individual, each conspiring witness receives the complete set of lashes.
Rava
The reason is simpler: "As he conspired to do to his brother" — their punishment must exactly mirror what they intended to do to the accused. If each received only a fraction of the lashes, the verse would not be fulfilled.
Bottom Line All agree: money penalties are divided equally among conspirators, but lashes are NOT divided — each witness receives the full 39 stripes.

👁️ How Witnesses Become Conspiring (עֵדִים זוֹמְמִין)

Set A testifies: "So-and-so murdered a person at this exact time and place"
↓
Set B contradicts the TESTIMONY
"The murder didn't happen" or "It was someone else"
Set B contradicts the WITNESSES
"You were with us in another city at that exact moment!"
↓
Only when Set B impeaches the witnesses themselves does Set A become conspiring witnesses (זוֹמְמִין). Contradicting their testimony alone does not.

💰 Dividing Punishment Among Conspiring Witnesses

Money Penalties

  • Divided equally among all conspiring witnesses
  • 3 conspirators → each pays one-third of the sum
  • Proportional — the group shares the financial burden
  • Logical: the harm to the victim's wallet is one unit

Lashes (מַלְקוֹת)

  • NOT divided — each witness receives the full 39 lashes
  • Cannot give a 'partial' flogging to satisfy the verse
  • Source (Abaye): gezera shava from the word 'wicked'
  • Source (Rava): 'as he conspired to do' demands a mirror punishment

📜 Rabbi Yehuda ben Tabbai's Tragic Mistake

עֵדִים זוֹמְמִין אֵינָן נֶהֱרָגִין עַד שֶׁיִּזּוֹמּוּ שְׁנֵיהֶם — Conspiring witnesses are not executed unless both of them are rendered conspiring
A Cautionary Tale Rabbi Yehuda ben Tabbai, seeking to refute the Sadducees, executed a single conspiring witness when only one of a pair was proven guilty — believing he was upholding strict justice.

Shimon ben Shataḥ rebuked him sharply: "You shed innocent blood! Conspiring witnesses are only executed when BOTH witnesses are found guilty together."

Rabbi Yehuda ben Tabbai was devastated. He accepted upon himself never to issue a halakhic ruling without Shimon ben Shataḥ's approval. He visited the grave of the slain witness daily and wept — and a voice would respond from the grave. His students consoled him: once he died, the voice would cease — meaning the voice came not from the executed witness but from the Divine response to his remorse.
