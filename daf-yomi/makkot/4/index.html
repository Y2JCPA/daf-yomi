⚖️ Conspiring Witnesses: Lashes + Payment?

Rabbi Meir
Conspiring witnesses who sought to impose lashes receive BOTH 80 lashes AND a monetary penalty — two sets of lashes from two separate prohibitions.
Rabbis
A court does not impose both flogging and monetary payment for the same act. They receive one or the other — lashes only, since that is what they sought.
Ulla
Rabbi Meir derives his ruling from the defamer (motzi shem ra): he is both flogged AND fined despite only using speech — so too conspiring witnesses.
Reish Lakish
The derivation runs the other way: Rabbi Yehuda learns that lav she'ein bo ma'aseh (speech-only prohibition) IS punishable by lashes — deriving from conspiring witnesses themselves.
Core Rule eidim zomemim receive the very punishment they conspired to impose on the accused — measure for measure

🌊 Barrel of Drawn Water in the Mediterranean

חָבִית שֶׁל שְׁאוּבִין שֶׁנָּפְלָה לַיָּם הַגָּדוֹל
Halakha — Rav (via Rav Yehuda) A barrel of drawn water (שְׁאוּבִין) that fell into the Mediterranean Sea: one who immerses at that very spot has NOT been purified. We fear the three log of drawn water remained concentrated and did not fully intermingle with the seawater. A baraita adds: the same applies to a barrel of wine — concern that three log of wine-colored liquid invalidated the spot.

📜 Can Speech-Only Prohibitions Incur Lashes?

Question
A lav she'ein bo ma'aseh — a prohibition violated by speech or inaction alone, with no physical deed — does it incur lashes? Normally lashes require active transgression.
Rabbi Yehuda
YES. Source: the defamer (motzi shem ra) — he only speaks, yet receives lashes AND pays a fine. Proves speech-based prohibition is punishable by lashes.
Objection
The defamer pays a kenas (fine) — making him an exceptional case. You cannot derive a general rule from a monetary penalty case!
Reish Lakish
Better source: conspiring witnesses themselves. Their sin is speech (false testimony), yet they are flogged. Derive lav she'ein bo ma'aseh from them instead.
Counter-objection
Conspiring witnesses ALSO pay a kenas! The combination of defamer + witnesses still fails — both involve fines, so neither can teach standard prohibitions.
Gemara's Struggle The derivation of a general rule for lav she'ein bo ma'aseh remains contested; every proposed source (defamer, conspiring witnesses) carries an exceptional kenas element that limits its reach.

🧠 Rav Yosef Forgets — Abaye Remembers

Rav Yosef declares: "I did not hear this halakha" — the ruling about whether the mishna's first clause includes the phrase 'less one kortov.'
↓
Abaye responds: "You yourself taught it to us — but you have forgotten it." He then recites the full teaching back to his master.
↓
Background: Rav Yosef suffered a severe illness (חוֹלִי) that caused significant memory loss (שִׁכְחָה). He forgot large portions of his learning.
↓
His students — especially Abaye — served as living repositories of Rav Yosef's own rulings, reciting them back to him as needed. The oral chain was preserved through the students.
