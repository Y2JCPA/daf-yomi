Horayot 12 Quiz

5 questions · Self-graded · Test your learning!
QUESTION 1 OF 5
Why does the Gemara say Jehoahaz could not have been anointed with the original anointing oil?
A. It had been destroyed in a fire
B. It was sequestered with the Ark by King Josiah before Jehoahaz's time
C. Only the first king of a dynasty could use it
D. The recipe had been lost after the First Temple was built
Correct: B — A baraita teaches that Josiah sequestered the Ark along with the anointing oil, the jar of manna, and Aaron's staff. Rav Pappa resolves that Jehoahaz was anointed with pure balsam oil instead.
QUESTION 2 OF 5
What shape is used when anointing a king, and what shape for a priest?
A. King: chi shape; Priest: crown shape
B. King: crown shape; Priest: chi (like Greek letter / Hebrew kaf)
C. Both are anointed in a crown shape
D. King: straight line on forehead; Priest: circle on head
Correct: B — Kings are anointed in the shape of a crown (nezer) around the head. Priests are anointed in the shape of the Greek letter chi, which Rav Menashya bar Gadda identifies as resembling the Hebrew letter kaf.
QUESTION 3 OF 5
Why were kings specifically anointed at a spring (ma'ayan)?
A. To purify the oil before application
B. As an omen that their kingdom would continue flowing like a spring
C. Because the original anointing oil was discovered near a spring
D. To ensure witnesses could observe the ceremony
Correct: B — The Gemara explains this was done as a siman (omen) — that the king's dynasty should endure and flow continuously like a spring. The source is Solomon's anointing at Gihon.
QUESTION 4 OF 5
According to Rabbi Yosei, what happens to a replacement High Priest after the original HP returns from his disqualification?
A. He serves alongside the original as a second High Priest
B. He returns to serving as an ordinary priest
C. He is unfit to serve as either High Priest or ordinary priest
D. He becomes the priest anointed for war
Correct: C — Rabbi Yosei holds the replacement is unfit for either role — not as HP due to enmity, and not as an ordinary priest because of the principle ma'alin ba-kodesh v'ein moridin (one elevates in sanctity and does not downgrade).
QUESTION 5 OF 5
Which category of High Priest is subject to only five halakhot from Leviticus 21?
A. The former (retired) High Priest
B. The High Priest consecrated by donning garments
C. The priest anointed for war (mashuaḥ milḥama)
D. The deputy High Priest (segan)
Correct: C — The baraita on Amud B teaches that the priest anointed for war is bound by only five matters: not growing hair long, not rending garments, not defiling for relatives, marrying a virgin, and not marrying a widow. Rabbi Yehuda adds that his death restores the murderer from the city of refuge, but the Rabbis disagree.
