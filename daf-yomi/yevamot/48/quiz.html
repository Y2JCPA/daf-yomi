Yevamot 48 Quiz

5 questions · Self-graded · Test your learning!
QUESTION 1 OF 5
According to Rabbi Eliezer, what must the Yefat Toar (captive woman) do with her nails?
A. Let them grow long
B. Cut them short
C. Paint them as a sign of mourning
D. Leave them completely unchanged
Correct: B — Rabbi Eliezer derives from the parallel with shaving the head: just as she removes hair from her head, so too she removes (cuts) her nails. Both acts diminish her beauty.
QUESTION 2 OF 5
How many days does Rabbi Shimon ben Elazar require the captive woman to mourn?
A. 30 days
B. 60 days
C. 90 days
D. 120 days
Correct: C — R. Shimon ben Elazar reads 'a month' (30) + 'days' (30 more) + 'after that' (another 30) = 90 days total. Ravina objects that this reasoning would produce an infinite regress.
QUESTION 3 OF 5
What does Rabbi Akiva say about maintaining an uncircumcised slave?
A. Permitted for up to twelve months
B. Permitted only if the slave was purchased conditionally
C. Forbidden, even for a single moment
D. Permitted indefinitely if the slave is a ger toshav
Correct: C — Rabbi Akiva holds one may not maintain an uncircumcised slave even momentarily. Rabbi Yishmael disagrees, holding such a slave may be kept while the master waits for compliance.
QUESTION 4 OF 5
According to Ravin citing Rabbi Ilai, which uncircumcised slave may be maintained indefinitely?
A. One who is elderly or infirm
B. One purchased on condition he would not be circumcised
C. One who previously accepted the Noahide laws
D. One whose master cannot afford the procedure
Correct: B — Rabbi Ilai rules that a slave purchased from the outset on explicit condition of non-circumcision may be kept — the condition was established at purchase and the master incurred no subsequent obligation.
QUESTION 5 OF 5
According to Rabbi Ḥananya son of Rabban Gamliel, why do converts suffer hardships?
A. They delayed their conversion too long
B. They do not yet know all the mitzvot
C. They failed to observe the seven Noahide mitzvot as gentiles
D. They are tested to strengthen their new faith
Correct: C — Rabbi Ḥananya teaches that converts are punished for having violated the Noahide mitzvot before converting. 'Others say' offer a different reason: they waited too long to convert.
