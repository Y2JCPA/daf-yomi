🧪 How Do We Test Whether a Perforation Truly Healed?

Rav Yosef
Bring warm barley bread and place it on his member — this will induce arousal and reveal whether the perforation has truly closed.
Abaye
Is everyone like our Patriarch Jacob, who never experienced an emission? We cannot rely on that. Instead, pass colorful women's garments before him to arouse him through sight.
Rava
Is everyone like Barzilai the Gileadite, who was exceptionally easily aroused? Perhaps not all men respond the same way — this method too may prove unreliable.
Core Ruling (Shmuel) If emission would reopen the healed perforation → the man remains unfit for the congregation. If the closure is permanent → he is fit.

Step 1: Take a sharp-edged grain of barley

👑 Saul's Court: Is David Fit for the Congregation?

Saul (inquiry)
Abner — whose son is this youth? David descends from Ruth the Moabite. Is he fit to enter the congregation? May my daughter marry him?
Abner
The verse says עמוני — 'an Ammonite man,' not woman! Female Moabites like Ruth are never included in the ban. David is fully fit!
Doeg
Then by your logic, 'an Egyptian' (masculine) should mean only Egyptian men are barred — yet we prohibit Egyptian women for three generations too!
Abner (rebuttal)
The cases differ: the Torah gives the reason for banning Ammonites — they did not bring bread and water to Israel. Women could not leave home to do so — the reason never applied to women at all!
⚖️ Halachic Resolution עמוני ולא עמונית — An Ammonite man, but not an Ammonite woman. Female Moabites are permitted. David and all his descendants are fit for the congregation. This was publicly established in Saul's generation.
