🔮 Who Was the Prophet Malachi?

Rav Naḥman
Malachi is Mordecai! He was called 'Malachi' because he was second to the king [melekh] — as recorded at the end of the Megilla.
R. Yehoshua ben Korḥa
No — Malachi is Ezra! It was Ezra who removed the foreign wives from among the Jews, precisely as Malachi prophesied.
The Rabbis
Malachi was his real name — he was a distinct prophet, neither Mordecai nor Ezra.
Rav Naḥman (revised)
It stands to reason that Malachi is Ezra — since Ezra's name is absent from the list of covenant signatories in Nehemiah, the text must be calling him by his other name: Malachi.
📌 Pattern of Hidden Identities The Gemara identifies multiple figures by alternate names: Hathach = Daniel (cut down from greatness); Malachi = Ezra (or Mordecai); Esther's royalty = the Shekhinah

💎 Four Beautiful Women of the World

Sarah

  • Beauty that surpassed all others
  • The archetypal figure of the four
  • Abimelech's blessing of Abraham fulfilled through her healing

Rahab

  • Aroused by her name alone
  • Ancestor of 8 prophets including Jeremiah
  • Harlot of Jericho who sheltered Israel's spies

Abigail

  • Aroused by remembering her
  • Wife of Nabal, then of David
  • Wisdom and beauty equally renowned

Esther

  • Listed by those who replace Vashti with Esther
  • Name alludes to nistar — the hidden
  • Queen who wore the Shekhinah into the throne room

🗣️ Rabbi Elazar in the Name of Rabbi Ḥanina — A Chain of Seven Teachings

1. The Shekhinah upon Esther: 'Clothed herself in royalty' — not garments but the Divine Presence that rested on her as she entered the court
↓
2. Blessings of ordinary people: Do not regard lightly — Abimelech blessed Abraham and Sarah was healed immediately
↓
3. Curses of ordinary people: Do not regard lightly — Abimelech's curse of Sarah ('covering of the eyes') was fulfilled in her descendants
↓
4. God's attribute vs. man's: Man lights fire then places the pot; God created light before the sun — the greater comes first with the Holy One
↓
5. Reporting in another's name brings redemption: Esther told the king in Mordecai's name → the plot of Bigthan and Teresh was foiled → world was saved
↓
6. A righteous person is lost only to his generation: Like a pearl lost at sea — it remains a pearl wherever it is; only the owner suffers its absence
↓
7. God will be a crown on each righteous person's head: 'The Lord of hosts shall be for a crown of glory' (Isaiah 28:5) — unique crown for each individual

😴 The King's Sleepless Night — Two Layers of Providence

"On that night the sleep of the king was disturbed" (Esther 6:1)
↓
Peshat — Ahasuerus
Suspected Haman and Esther were conspiring against him — then concluded: surely Mordecai saved my life and received nothing!
Derash — King of Universe
Rabbi Tanḥum: God's sleep was disturbed — the Holy One, Blessed be He, set the miraculous chain in motion
↓
Records were read by themselves — passive voice 'they were read' signals miraculous self-turning pages
↓
'It was found written [katuv]' — Mordecai's unrewarded deed miraculously surfaced at precisely this moment
↓
Stage set for Haman's public humiliation and Mordecai's elevation
