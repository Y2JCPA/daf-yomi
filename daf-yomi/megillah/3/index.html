📜 The Aramaic Targum: From Ezra to Yonatan

Ezra's Assembly read Torah to the returnees: "they gave the sense" = Targum (Nehemiah 8:8) — the tradition was first established then
↓
Onkelos the Convert re-established the Aramaic Targum of the Torah under the guidance of Rabbi Eliezer and Rabbi Yehoshua — he had forgotten the ancient tradition and restored it
↓
Yonatan ben Uzziel translated the Prophets — causing Eretz Yisrael to quake 400 × 400 parsangs and a Heavenly Voice to cry out
↓
Yonatan declared: I revealed Your secrets not for my own honor, nor for my father's house — but for Your honor alone, to prevent strife in Israel
↓
A Divine Voice told him: "Enough! Do not translate the Writings" — they contain hidden End of Days secrets (e.g., Zechariah 12:11)

😇 The Men Who Fled — Invisible Vision

Verse (Daniel 10:7)
Daniel alone saw the vision. The men with him did not see the vision — yet great trembling fell upon them and they fled to hide.
Question
If they did not see the vision, why were they frightened?
Gemara
Although they themselves did not see the vision, their guardian angels (מַזָּלָם) saw it — and so they felt an overwhelming, sourceless dread.
Ravina
Learn from this: one who is frightened for no apparent reason — his guardian angel sees something. He should recite Shema, change his location, or recite a special formula to ward off danger.
Gemara (identity)
The men with Daniel were Haggai, Zechariah, and Malachi — they were greater than Daniel in that they were true prophets; Daniel was greater than them in that he saw the vision while they did not.
Principle Unseen spiritual realities affect the physical world — guardian angels perceive what humans cannot

⚔️ Joshua at Jericho — The Angel's Double Rebuke

The Encounter

  • Joshua sees a figure with a drawn sword
  • Asks: 'Are you for us or our enemies?'
  • Angel: 'I am captain of God's host — I have come now'
  • Joshua bows: 'What does my lord command his servant?'
  • Why did Joshua bow to an unidentified figure? Demons never invoke God's name falsely — so Joshua knew this was genuine

The Two Failures

  • ❶ Yesterday (afternoon): neglected the תָּמִיד — afternoon daily offering
  • ❷ Tonight: neglecting Torah study with the people
  • Joshua spent the rest of the night בְּעֻמְקָא דְהִלְכְתָא — in the depths of halacha
  • Rav Shmuel bar Unya: Torah study of the masses > daily offerings
  • Sages of Rabbi Yehuda HaNasi's house: individual Torah study < Temple service

🔍 Why the Prophets' Targum Caused the Earth to Quake

וְאִילְמָלֵא תַּרְגּוּמוֹ שֶׁל אוֹנְקְלוֹס לֹא יָדַעְנוּ מַה הוּא אוֹמֵר
Rav Yosef on Zechariah 12:11 "The mourning of Hadadrimmon in the valley of Megiddon" — were it not for the Aramaic Targum of this verse, we would not know what it means at all, since no incident is mentioned in the Bible. The Targum reveals: this refers to two separate mournings — the mourning for Ahab son of Omri, killed by Hadadrimmon ben Tabrimmon, and the mourning for Josiah son of Amon, killed by Pharaoh Necho in the valley of Megiddo. These are End of Days secrets — hence the quaking when Yonatan revealed them.
