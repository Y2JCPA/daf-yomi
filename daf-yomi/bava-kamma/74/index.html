Is Contradiction the Beginning of Hazamah?

Rava
Contradiction of testimony (hakchashah) is the start of determining testimony is conspiring (hazamah)
Abaye
Contradiction and hazamah are independent — contradiction alone does not begin the hazamah process
R' Elazar
Contradicted witnesses in capital cases are flogged but not killed — even if later rendered conspiring
R' Yochanan
Contradicted witnesses who are later rendered conspiring are killed — contradiction is the start of hazamah
Core Issue When witnesses are first contradicted and then rendered conspiring, does the earlier contradiction count as part of the hazamah process — or does it disqualify the testimony before hazamah can apply?

The Baraita's Case: Slave Injury Order

First witnesses testify: Master knocked out tooth first, then blinded eye
↓
Result: Slave goes free for tooth; master pays eye value to slave
↓
Second witnesses render first pair edim zomemim and reverse the order
↓
True Order (per 2nd set)
Eye blinded first → slave freed for eye. Then tooth knocked out → no additional payment
Conspiring Witnesses Pay
They pay value of an eye to the master — the extra liability they tried to impose
Key Detail Both sets of witnesses agree injuries occurred — they disagree only about the sequence, which changes who pays what

Abaye's Required Setup for the Baraita

📋 Master injures slave (both tooth and eye) — order disputed
↓
⚖️ Master stands trial in first court — court determines liability
↓
🏃 Master evades payment and flees
↓
🏛️ Master brought before second court — witnesses testify about first trial's result
↓
🔍 Second set of witnesses renders first set zomemim and reverses the order
↓
💰 Conspiring witnesses pay the difference in liability (eye value) to master
Why So Complex? If the master hadn't already stood trial, the conspiring witnesses would owe the entire slave value — since at testimony time, the master had no prior liability

Rava's Proof: Contradiction as Start of Hazamah

Rav Idi bar Avin
Our Mishna (72b) supports Rava: witnesses testified to theft and slaughter, rendered conspiring — they pay everything (4x/5x payment)
Rav Idi bar Avin
Once rendered zomemim on theft, slaughter testimony is effectively contradicted (no theft = no slaughter penalty). Yet they still pay!
Rav Idi bar Avin
This proves contradiction is the start of hazamah — otherwise the slaughter testimony would be nullified before hazamah!
Sages
No proof! The mishna may refer to where they were rendered zomemim on slaughter first, then theft — so slaughter testimony was never merely contradicted
The Refutation By reordering when each testimony was rendered conspiring, the Sages eliminate the proof — the slaughter hazamah preceded the theft hazamah

R' Yochanan vs. R' Elazar: Contradicted Capital Witnesses

R' Yochanan

  • Contradicted then rendered conspiring → witnesses ARE killed
  • Contradiction = start of hazamah
  • Full conspiring witness penalty applies

R' Elazar

  • Contradicted then rendered conspiring → witnesses are NOT killed
  • Contradiction ≠ start of hazamah
  • Contradicted capital witnesses receive flogging only
Proof for R' Elazar R' Elazar rules contradicted capital witnesses are flogged. If they could later be killed (via hazamah), flogging wouldn't apply — a prohibition carrying the death penalty cannot also carry flogging (lav she-nitten l'azharas misat beit din).
