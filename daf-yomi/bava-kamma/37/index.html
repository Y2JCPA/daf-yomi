Forewarning: How Specific Is It?

An ox gores three times
under specific conditions
↓
Is it forewarned only for those conditions
or for all similar situations?
Rav Zevid
Default: forewarned for all species
Mishna specifies only the proven innocuous case
Rav Pappa
Default: NOT forewarned for other species
Forewarning is species-specific
Core Issue Does forewarning for one category automatically extend to related categories, or must each be established independently?

Orphans & the Prosbol

יתומים אין צריכין פרוזבול
Opening Ruling Orphans do not require a prosbol (document preventing Sabbatical Year debt cancellation) because the court is considered their steward (apotropos). Their debts are automatically transferred to the court.

Rami bar Ḥama taught: Rabban Gamliel and his court were like fathers to orphans — and all subsequent courts inherit this status.

How to Read the Mishna: Default Forewarning Status

Rav Zevid
The mishna reads: 'forewarned for its own species but not forewarned for other species' — meaning the ox was proven innocuous for other species. By default, forewarning extends to all species.
Rav Pappa
The mishna reads: 'forewarned for its own species is not forewarned for other species' — meaning forewarning for one species does not automatically apply to others.
Rav Zevid
My proof: The mishna also says 'forewarned for small but not for large.' This only adds something new if by default forewarning extends broadly — even from small to large!
Rav Pappa
The latter clause teaches a separate principle — even within the same species, forewarning for small doesn't extend to large. Each category must be independently established.
Key Dispute Is forewarning expansive by default (Rav Zevid) or narrow by default (Rav Pappa)?

Establishing a Cycle: Ox Goring vs. Menstrual Veset

Rav

  • Ox gores on 15th, 16th, 17th of consecutive months
  • Pattern is established: a month + one day
  • Three instances suffice to set the cycle
  • Same logic applies to a woman's menstrual veset

Shmuel

  • Three instances only show a possible pattern
  • Cycle not established until the skip repeats three times
  • Requires a 4th month (goring on 18th) to confirm
  • The pattern must be proven, not merely suggested
Parallel The Gemara links ox forewarning cycles to the laws of niddah — the same logic of pattern-establishment governs both.
