Pebbles (Tzrorot): The Hybrid Damage Category

Sumakhos
Pebbles propelled by an animal — the owner pays full damage (nezek shalem)
Rabbis
Pebbles propelled by an animal — the owner pays only half damage (chatzi nezek)
Rava
New dilemma: Is payment for pebbles from the body of the animal or from superior-quality land?
Rava
Further dilemma: Is there forewarning (ha'adah) for propelling pebbles, or does the animal always pay half?
Core Tension Tzrorot (pebbles) are a hybrid — the animal walks normally (like Trampling) but the damage is indirect (like Goring). The Gemara struggles to classify this in-between category.

Sumakhos vs. Rabbis on Pebbles

Sumakhos
An animal propels pebbles while walking normally — owner pays full damage (nezek shalem)
Rabbis
Normal walking that propels pebbles — owner pays only half damage (chatzi nezek)
Gemara
The baraita about chickens and the bucket — is it Sumakhos or the Rabbis?
Gemara
If Sumakhos: why does the seifa say half for the second vessel? He holds full for all pebbles!
Rav Ashi
My unresolved dilemma: Does Sumakhos distinguish koach from koach kocho?
Unresolved The Gemara cannot definitively attribute the baraita to either Sumakhos or the Rabbis. Rav Beivai bar Abaye offers an alternative reading, and the dilemma remains unresolved (teiku).

Koach vs. Koach Kocho — Direct vs. Secondary Force

Koach (Direct Force)

  • Animal propels pebbles while walking
  • Chicken pecks rope → rope breaks
  • Sumakhos: Full damage
  • Rabbis: Half damage
  • Subcategory of Trampling (Regel)

Koach Kocho (Secondary Force)

  • Bucket falls → shard hits another vessel
  • Force generated by a force
  • Even Sumakhos may concede: Half damage
  • Rav Ashi's dilemma left unresolved
  • Further removed from the animal's action
Principle The more steps removed from the animal's direct action, the weaker the owner's liability. But the exact boundaries remain debated.

From body of animal (mi-gufo)We never find half-damage paid from superior landLike Goring (Keren) — innocuous ox pays from its body
From superior land (min ha-aliyah)We never find normal-manner damage paid from the bodyLike Trampling (Regel) — forewarned from the start, paid from land

The Hybrid Problem Pebbles behave like Trampling (normal walking) but are treated like Goring (half damage). Which payment mechanism applies?
