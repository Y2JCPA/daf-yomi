✍️ Who Wrote the Last 8 Verses of the Torah?

Rabbi Yehuda
Joshua wrote the last eight verses, since Moses could not have written the account of his own death. It is impossible that Moses wrote 'And Moses died there.'
Rabbi Shimon
Is it possible the Torah was missing even a single letter? God commanded Moses: 'Put this Torah scroll by the Ark' — it must have been complete. Rather, Moses wrote these verses with tears instead of ink.
Gemara
Rav's ruling that these verses are read by one reader fits Rabbi Shimon — they remain 'Torah of Moses,' not a separate composition begun by Joshua.
📖 Verse Deuteronomy 34:5 — 'And Moses the servant of the Lord died there'

📏 Physical Requirements for a Torah Scroll Sheet

Columns per sheet: minimum 3, maximum 8 — writing 8+ narrow columns resembles a personal letter (missive), which is forbidden
↓
9-column sheet: split into 4+5 — never 3+6, as three columns alone is already the minimum; both resulting sheets must each have at least three columns
↓
Final sheet exception: the last sheet of the scroll may contain even a single verse, or even a single letter
↓
Lower margin: 1 tefaḥ (handbreadth) — large enough to prevent a reader's arm from resting on the writing
↓
Upper margin: ⅔ tefaḥ | Between columns: 2 finger-breadths | Beginning sheet margin: 3 finger-breadths
↓
Line spacing: equal to one full line of text | Between words: one small letter | Between letters: a hairsbreadth

✡️ Three Opinions: Scribe Omits God's Name Mid-Text

Rabbi Yosei

  • Scribe omits God's name, then writes the next word
  • Scrape off whatever was written after the omission
  • Suspend the scraped words above the line
  • Write God's name in the now-empty space below
  • The suspended words hover above the correction

Rabbi Shimon Shezuri

  • May suspend the entire God's name above the line
  • May NOT suspend only part of the name (e.g., two of four letters above, two below)
  • The divine name must appear whole — never split across positions

Rabbi Shimon ben Elazar (R. Meir)

  • May NOT suspend the name above the line at all — neither partial nor whole
  • Must scrape and rewrite the entire section properly
  • No suspension permitted under any circumstances

📝 Word Doesn't Fit in the Column?

Scribe reaches end of column with a word that won't fit
↓
5-letter word
Write 3 letters inside column, 2 in margin
❌ NOT 2 inside, 3 outside
2-letter word
Do NOT split it into the gutter space between columns
The inter-column space is not writing space
↓
Rule: column integrity must be maintained — margins and gutters are never legitimate writing areas
