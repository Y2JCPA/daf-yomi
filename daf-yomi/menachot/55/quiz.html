QUESTION 2 OF 5
What is Rav Pappa's interpretive principle stated in this daf?
A. When two Tannaim disagree, we follow the later one
B. We prefer interpreting a mishna under two different situations (one Tanna) over attributing it to two Tannaim

Correct: B. Rav Pappa says: "We exert ourselves and interpret a mishna according to two reasons (scenarios) under one Tanna rather than attributing it to two different Tannaim." Single authorship is always preferred.
