Menachot 42 — Quiz

1. How do you test tekhelet dye?

✅ b) Soak in 40-day-old snail solution overnight. If the color holds, it's genuine. If it fades, it's plant-based counterfeit.

2. Why is there "no reliable test" for tekhelet?

✅ b) Some plant dyes (kala ilan) produce equally permanent blue — the test can't distinguish the source, only the quality.

3. Is a 3-cornered garment obligated in tzitzit?

✅ b) A garment with only 3 corners is exempt from tzitzit. The minimum is 4 corners.

4. What are the three daily blessings of distinction?

✅ b) R. Meir: gratitude for not being made a gentile, a woman, or a slave — expressing gratitude for mitzva obligations.

5. From whom should you buy tekhelet?

✅ b) Since there's no definitive test for the dye source, you must buy only from a reputable, established dealer you can trust.
