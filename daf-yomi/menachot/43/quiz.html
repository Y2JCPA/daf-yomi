Menachot 43 — Quiz

1. What is the chilazon?

✅ b) A sea creature resembling a fish, emerging every 70 years, whose blood is used for the sky-blue tekhelet dye.

2. What chain of resemblance does tekhelet evoke?

✅ b) Tekhelet → sea → sky → sapphire → Throne of Glory. Looking at the strings leads to contemplating God's sovereignty.

3. R. Natan says there is no mitzva that is:

✅ b) No mitzva is minor — even tzitzit (seemingly small) leads to remembering all the commandments. Every mitzva has reward.

4. After the long digression, what topic does the Gemara return to?

✅ b) The Gemara returns to the core topic of menachot — flour, oil, and libations that accompany animal sacrifices.

5. Are meal offerings and libations indispensable for animal sacrifices?

✅ b) The verse "and their meal offerings and their libations" teaches that each animal's accompanying meal offering and libation is indispensable.
