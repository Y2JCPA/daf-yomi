Menachot 38 — Quiz

1. Is the uppermost knot of tzitzit Torah law or rabbinic?

✅ b) Torah law — Rabba proves it from the fact that we can wear tzitzit in a cemetery.

2. A string severed above the knot — fit or unfit?

✅ b) If severed above the knot and enough string remains to tie a small knot, the tzitzit remain fit.

3. Can tzitzit be entirely windings with no loose strings?

✅ b) Rav ruled them invalid. Must have both windings (gedilim) AND loose strings (petilim).

4. What is the ratio of windings to loose strings?

✅ b) One-third windings (near the garment) and two-thirds loose strings hanging below.

5. Are knots between sets of windings required?

✅ c) The sons of R. Chiyya had them, but the Gemara couldn't conclusively prove they are required.
