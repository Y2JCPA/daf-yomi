Menachot 41 — Quiz

1. What is the minimum length for tzitzit strings?

✅ b) Minimum of about 4 thumb-widths — enough to tie a small knot with the hanging strings.

2. Why doesn't a sofer recite a blessing when writing tefillin?

✅ a) The writing is preparation; the mitzva is completed by wearing them. Blessings are for the completion of the mitzva.

3. What did the angel say to Rav Katina?

✅ b) The angel warned that in times of divine anger, even failing to fulfill a positive commandment (from which one is technically exempt) can lead to punishment.

4. Can a woman write a Torah scroll?

✅ b) A Torah scroll written by a woman is invalid, along with scrolls written by heretics, gentiles, minors, Samaritans, slaves, and apostates.

5. Where does the word "tzitzit" come from?

✅ b) From Ezekiel 8:3: "took me by a lock (tzitzit) of my head" — implies something that hangs and dangles.
