TypeDescriptionUsed For

Open & Closed Sections (Parsha Petucha/Stuma)

Petucha (Open)

  • Starts at the beginning of a new line
  • Previous line ends mid-line with a gap
  • Like a new paragraph

Stuma (Closed)

  • Starts mid-line after a small gap
  • Continues on the same line as the previous section
  • Like a sentence break within a paragraph
