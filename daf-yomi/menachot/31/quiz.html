Menachot 31 — Quiz

1. Which parchment type is used for a mezuza?

✅ b) Mezuza is written on duchsustos — the inner (flesh-facing) side of the split hide.

2. Can a Torah scroll be cut to make a mezuza?

✅ b) Ma'alin bakodesh v'ein moridin — we elevate in sanctity but don't reduce. Torah > tefillin > mezuza.

3. What is a parsha petucha (open section)?

✅ b) A petucha (open section) starts at the beginning of a new line, with the previous line ending mid-line with a gap.

4. What spacing does the mezuza require?

✅ b) The mezuza requires space above, space below, and the two passages should be written in an open manner (second passage on a new line).

5. Whose mezuza practice serves as the model?

✅ c) R. Shimon b. Elazar describes R. Meir's mezuza writing practice as the model — written like a column with proper margins.
