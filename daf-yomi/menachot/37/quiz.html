Menachot 37 — Quiz

1. Can you fulfill the mitzva of tzitzit with only white strings?

✅ b) The Mishna teaches that white strings and sky-blue strings don't prevent each other — each fulfills the mitzva independently.

2. According to R. Shimon, what is the relationship between white and blue strings?

✅ b) R. Shimon holds white strings and sky-blue strings are two entirely separate mitzvot.

3. What does "ta'aseh v'lo min ha'asui" mean?

✅ b) "Make it, and not from what is already made" — the strings must be intentionally attached as tzitzit, not pre-existing on the garment.

4. When can an aseh override a lo ta'aseh?

✅ c) A Torah-level positive commandment can override a rabbinic-level prohibition, including "lo tasur."

5. According to Rav, what did the Mishna mean by "each doesn't prevent the other"?

✅ b) Rav explains: you fulfilled A mitzva, but not the complete mitzva — like wearing only the arm tefillin without the head.
