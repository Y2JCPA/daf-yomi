Menachot 44 — Quiz

1. The "rams" that prevent each other refer to which festival?

✅ b) Shavuot — the two rams prescribed in Leviticus 23:19 as peace offerings with the two loaves.

2. On Sukkot, if you have 12 bulls instead of 13 on day 1:

✅ b) Individual additional bulls of Sukkot are not indispensable to each other — sacrifice what you have.

3. Do the two loaves prevent the two sheep on Shavuot?

✅ b) The loaves are primary — their absence prevents the sheep. But the sheep's absence does not prevent the loaves.

4. What should you do if you have rams but not enough flour?

✅ b) Better to sacrifice fewer animals with complete meal offerings than more animals without flour. The flour is indispensable.

5. How many separate sets of offerings does Shavuot have?

✅ b) Two separate sets — Leviticus 23 (including the two loaves and sheep) and Numbers 28 (Musaf additional offerings).
