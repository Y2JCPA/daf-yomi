If the Mishna says "bulls" prevent each other ‚Äî does it mean all bulls on Sukkot (which start at 13 and decrease daily)?

What If They Ran Out of Flour?

Scenario: The community has enough rams for the festival but not enough flour for the accompanying meal offerings.

Ruling: They should not sacrifice the rams without the flour. The meal offerings are indispensable ‚Äî better to sacrifice fewer animals with complete meal offerings than more animals without.
