Can Apple Juice Leaven Dough?

  • Cannot be used for todah/two loaves
  • But it does create nukshe (hardened leaven)

R. Chanina ben Gamliel

  • Apple juice does leaven
  • Can be used for leavened meal offerings
  • (Rav Kahana attributes to R. Chanina b. Teradyon)

The Sinner's Meal Offering: Can You Add Water?

The minchat chotet (sinner's meal offering) has no oil. How does the kohen remove a proper handful from dry flour?

R. Ila

  • Hardest kemitza of all offerings
  • "Dry" means dry of everything
  • No oil AND no water
  • Flour falls through fingers

Rav Yitzchak bar Avdimi

  • Not particularly difficult
  • "Dry" means dry of oil only
  • May knead with water
  • Dough holds together fine
