Menachot 54 — Quiz

1. According to the majority view, what does apple juice do to dough?

✅ b) The Rabbis hold apple juice does not create full chametz, but it does create nukshe (hardened leaven), which is significant for teruma purposes.

2. What does "dry" (חֲרֵיבָה) mean regarding the sinner's meal offering, according to R. Ila?

✅ b) R. Ila holds "dry" means dry of all substances — no oil and no water — making the kemitza (removal of a handful) the most difficult of all offerings.

3. In the dispute about measuring food, who holds "as they are" (current state)?

✅ b) Rav, R. Chiyya, and R. Yochanan hold that we measure food as it currently is. Shmuel, R. Shimon bar Rabbi, and Reish Lakish hold we measure as it originally was.

4. Food that was below shiur, then swelled to shiur — what is its status according to Rabba?

✅ b) Rabba's synthesis: if it was initially insufficient but now sufficient, it is susceptible to impurity by rabbinic law only.

5. The Gemara conclusively refutes which position?

✅ a) The Gemara says "teyuvta" — a conclusive refutation of the view that there IS permanent disqualification in ritual matters, based on the mishna showing food can regain impurity after shrinking and swelling back.
