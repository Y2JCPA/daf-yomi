What if the Kohen intends to consume something that's normally BURNED,
or to burn something that's normally EATEN?
Intent Example R' Eliezer Chachamim
Eat what's normally burned Eat the Kometz tomorrow Pasul Kosher
Burn what's normally eaten Burn the Shirayim tomorrow Pasul Kosher

Key principle: For full Piggul (with Kares), the intent must match the item's normal use. "Cross-category" intent (eating what's normally burned) disqualifies according to R' Eliezer, but doesn't reach Piggul level.
