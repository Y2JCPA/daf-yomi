Menachot 28 — Quiz

1. Why were Moses's trumpets unfit for future generations?

✅ b) "Make for you" teaches that the trumpets were personal to Moses and each generation must fashion their own.

2. How many goblets does the Menorah have?

✅ c) 22 goblets: 4 on the main shaft and 3 on each of the 6 branches (18), totaling 22.

3. If the community cannot afford gold, what can the Menorah be made from?

✅ b) If the community is poor, the Menorah may be made from any metal. The decorative goblets, knobs, and flowers are not required in that case.

4. According to Rav, how tall is the Menorah itself?

✅ b) Rav says 9 handbreadths. The Tamid mishna's 18 includes 3 stone steps that are not part of the Menorah itself.

5. Are the seven lamps indispensable even if the Menorah is not gold?

✅ b) Seven lamps are indispensable regardless of the material. The decorative elements are optional for non-gold, but the structure of seven lamps is always required.
