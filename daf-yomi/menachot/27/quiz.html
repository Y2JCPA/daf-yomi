Menachot 27 — Quiz

1. What does "minority prevents majority" mean for the handful?

✅ b) If any part of the handful is missing, the entire burning of the handful is invalid and cannot render the remainder permitted.

2. How many species are indispensable in the lulav bundle?

✅ c) Four species: lulav, etrog, hadas (myrtle), and arava (willow). Absence of any one prevents fulfillment of the mitzva.

3. What happens if red heifer sprinklings are not aimed precisely toward the Tent of Meeting?

✅ c) The Rabbis hold imprecise aim invalidates the red heifer sprinklings; R. Yehuda holds they are valid as long as the intent was correct.

4. According to the Rabbis, entering which area of the Temple incurs death at Heaven's hand?

✅ b) The Rabbis hold that entering within the Curtain (including before the Aron Cover) incurs death at Heaven's hand, while entering the Sanctuary incurs lashes.

5. What is the source for the oil of a voluntary meal offering being indispensable?

✅ b) The possessive "of its oil" (Lev. 2:2) teaches that if any amount of the oil is missing, the offering is not valid.
