Entering the Sanctuary — Zones of Holiness

The Rabbis

  • Sanctuary (Heichal): "He shall not come" → 40 lashes
  • Within the Curtain + Before the Aron: "He shall not die" → Death at Heaven's hand

R. Yehuda

  • Sanctuary + Within the Curtain: "He shall not come" → 40 lashes
  • Before the Aron Cover only: "He shall not die" → Death at Heaven's hand
The verse (Lev. 16:2): "That he not come at all times into the holy place, within the Curtain, before the Ark Cover, that he not die." The dispute: where does "shall not come" end and "shall not die" begin?

Key Takeaways — Menachot 27

1. The "me'akev" principle: if any part of a required component is missing, the whole offering is invalid.

2. Handful, flour, wine, oil — each must be complete. Flour+oil and handful+frankincense are interdependent pairs.

3. Grand catalog: from 2-item pairs (Yom Kippur goats) through 4-item groups (lulav, todah loaves, leper purification).

4. Red heifer: wrong intent always invalidates; imprecise aim disputed (Rabbis vs. R. Yehuda).

5. Sanctuary zones: Rabbis draw the death-penalty line at the Curtain; R. Yehuda draws it at the Aron Cover itself.
