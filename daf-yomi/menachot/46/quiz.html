Menachot 46 — Quiz

1. If the loaves are lost after waving, what happens to the sheep?

✅ b) The sheep revert to their individual olah status — they are still valid offerings even without the loaves.

2. Are the Shavuot two loaves and two sheep a communal or individual offering?

✅ b) A communal obligation brought on behalf of the entire Jewish people.

3. If Shavuot falls on Shabbat, when are the sheep offered?

✅ c) When Shavuot falls on Shabbat, the sheep are offered on Sunday. The loaves are baked Friday and valid through Shabbat.

4. If Shavuot passes without bringing the offerings, can they be made up later?

✅ b) Once Shavuot passes, the communal obligation lapses — the loaves and sheep cannot be brought afterward.

5. What topic bridges daf 46 to daf 47?

✅ b) The Kohen Gadol's chavitin offering — who brings it when he dies, how it's funded — bridges directly from the end of the Shavuot discussion into the chavitin section starting at daf 47.
