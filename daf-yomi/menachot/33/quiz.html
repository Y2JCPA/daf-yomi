Menachot 33 — Quiz

1. Is a gatehouse leading only to a garden required to have a mezuza?

✅ b) Exempt — the gatehouse is made for reaching the garden, not as a dwelling entrance.

2. How does R. Akiva derive 4 compartments for head tefillin?

✅ b) R. Akiva derives it linguistically: "tot" means 2 in Katpi and "fot" means 2 in Afriki, totaling 4.

3. Head tefillin: how many parchments?

✅ c) Head tefillin require 4 separate parchments, one for each passage, each in its own compartment.

4. Arm tefillin: how many parchments?

✅ c) Arm tefillin have all 4 passages written on a single parchment in one compartment.

5. For a ceiling aperture, where does the mezuza go?

✅ b) The mezuza goes on the right side as one ascends, since the upper dwelling is primary and the person ascending is "entering."
