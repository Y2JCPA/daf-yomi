Menachot 45 — Quiz

1. According to R. Yochanan, what creates the bond between the loaves and sheep?

✅ b) R. Yochanan holds that slaughter of the sheep creates the definitive bond between the loaves and the sheep.

2. If the sheep are lost BEFORE slaughter (after waving), what happens?

✅ b) Before slaughter, the bond is not yet complete. Bring new sheep and wave with the original loaves again.

3. What is R. Yirmeya's real dilemma?

✅ b) R. Yirmeya's dilemma: if sheep are lost after waving, can the already-waved loaves be used with new sheep, or must new loaves also be brought?

4. After slaughter — sheep are lost. What happens to the loaves?

✅ b) After slaughter the bond is complete — losing either means losing both. New loaves and new sheep must be brought.

5. Who raised the original dilemma about what establishes the bond?

✅ b) Ulla (in the West, Eretz Yisrael) raised the dilemma about whether waving or slaughter establishes the bond.
