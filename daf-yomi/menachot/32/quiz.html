Menachot 32 — Quiz

1. Which side of the doorpost gets the mezuza?

✅ b) The mezuza goes on the right side as you enter the room.

2. Why place it near the outside edge?

✅ b) Rava says it's a mitzva to place it in the handbreadth closest to the public domain so the whole house is guarded.

3. How does R. Chanina contrast God and human kings?

✅ b) A human king sits inside while servants guard from outside. HKB"H's servants (Israel) sit inside while He guards them from outside through the mezuza.

4. A bathhouse requires a mezuza. True or false?

✅ b) Bathhouses are exempt from mezuza because they are not used for dignified dwelling.

5. What height should the mezuza be placed at?

✅ c) The mezuza should be placed in the upper third of the doorpost, but not at the very top — at least a handbreadth below.
