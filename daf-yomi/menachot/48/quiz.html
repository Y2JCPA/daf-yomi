If the two lambs of Shavuot were slaughtered with four loaves instead of two, how does Rav Chisda say to redeem the extra loaves according to Rebbi?
A. Redeem them outside the Azarah
B. Redeem them inside the Azarah ‚Äî they became Chulin automatically
C. Burn them on the Mizbeach
D. Leave them until Ibur Tzurah, then burn them
Correct: B. Rav Chisda says they are redeemed inside the Azarah. Even though they become Chulin, it's not considered "bringing Chulin into the Azarah" because the loaves were already there as Kodesh and became Chulin automatically through the redemption.
QUESTION 2 OF 5
R' Chanina Tirata taught that if four lambs were slaughtered for two loaves, you should first do Zerikah on two of them Lo Lishmah. Why?
A. Because Lo Lishmah Zerikah is always preferable
B. To avoid losing the other two lambs ‚Äî if you do Lishmah first, the remaining pair becomes Nidcheh (rejected)
C. Because all four lambs must have Zerikah done on the same day
D. Because the Kohen cannot distinguish between the lambs
Correct: B. If you do Zerikah Lishmah first on two lambs, the other two become Nidchim ‚Äî they were fit for Lishmah, but now the obligation is fulfilled and they can't even be offered as Shelamim. By doing Lo Lishmah first, you save all four.
QUESTION 3 OF 5
What is R' Yochanan's objection to R' Chanina's approach of doing Zerikah Lo Lishmah first?
A. Lo Lishmah Zerikah is never valid
B. The Torah requires Lishmah Zerikah to come first
C. "Amod v'Chatei" ‚Äî we don't tell someone to sin (do Lo Lishmah) in order to gain (save the other lambs)
D. Four lambs cannot be offered on Shavuot under any circumstances
Correct: C. R' Yochanan invokes the principle of "Amod v'Chatei b'Shvil she'Tizkeh" ‚Äî we don't instruct someone to actively transgress (Zerikah Lo Lishmah) just to produce a benefit (saving the extra lambs).
QUESTION 4 OF 5
In the Mishnah of the broken barrel of Terumah wine falling into impure Chulin wine below, what does R' Yehoshua permit?
A. Drinking the mixture
B. Pouring out all the wine
C. Actively making the Terumah wine Tamei (catching it in a Tamei vessel) to save the Chulin wine below
D. Adding more Chulin wine to dilute the mixture
Correct: C. R' Yehoshua says you may actively be Metamei the Terumah (catch it in an impure vessel) to prevent it from falling into and forbidding all the Chulin wine below. The Gemara distinguishes this case: since the Terumah was going to become Tamei anyway, it's not really "sinning to gain."

Rav Yitzchak and Tanna d'Vei Levi disagree about Kivsei Atzeres slaughtered Lo Lishmah. What is the basis of their machloket?
A. Whether Shavuot offerings require Semichah
B. Whether the sheep are compared to a Chatas (disqualified Lo Lishmah) or to Shalmei Nedavah (valid Lo Lishmah)
C. Whether the loaves must accompany the sheep
D. Whether the sheep must be less than one year old
Correct: B. Rav Yitzchak equates the sheep to a Chatas via a hekesh ‚Äî so Lo Lishmah makes them Pasul. Tanna d'Vei Levi derives Shalmei Chovah (obligatory peace offerings) from Shalmei Nedavah (voluntary peace offerings) ‚Äî so Lo Lishmah keeps them Kosher, just doesn't fulfill the obligation.
