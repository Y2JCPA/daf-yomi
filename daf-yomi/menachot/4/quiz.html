According to Rav, Minchas Ha'Omer from which Kemitzah was done Lo Lishmah is Pasul. Why doesn't the Mishnah list it among the exceptions?
A. The Mishnah forgot about it
B. The Mishnah only lists individual (not communal) offerings, those brought independently (not with a Zevach), and those with no fixed time
C. Rav's ruling was given after the Mishnah was compiled

Correct: B. The Mishnah only teaches about Menachot that are (1) brought by individuals (not communal like the Omer), (2) brought independently (not accompanying a Zevach), and (3) have no fixed time. The Omer fails all three criteria.
