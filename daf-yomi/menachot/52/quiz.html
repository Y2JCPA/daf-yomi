Menachot 52 — Quiz

1. Why did the Sages first decree me'ila (misuse of consecrated property) on red heifer ashes?

✅ b) People were treating the ashes disrespectfully and making medicinal salves for their wounds from them, so the Sages decreed me'ila to prevent misuse.

2. When the KG dies without replacement, how much flour is brought for the chavitin?

✅ c) A baraita explicitly states: complete tenth in the morning and complete tenth in the afternoon, derived from "סֹלֶת מִנְחָה תָּמִיד" — it parallels the daily offerings.

3. According to Abba Yosei ben Dostai, how much frankincense does the KG normally bring with his chavitin?

✅ c) Abba Yosei ben Dostai holds the KG separates two handfuls of frankincense daily — one for morning, one for afternoon — because we never find half a handful being sacrificed.

4. Which R. Yirmeya quote appears in this daf?

✅ b) R. Yirmeya dismisses Rava's proof from the Tamid mishna, calling the Babylonians foolish for deriving halakhot from what-if cases that the Tanna never intended to address.

5. When the KG dies, is the oil for the chavitin doubled?

✅ b) The oil remains 3 log, split 1.5 and 1.5. What the Torah revealed as doubled (flour) is doubled; what it did not reveal (oil, frankincense) is not doubled.
