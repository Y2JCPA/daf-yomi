Menachot 35 — Quiz

1. If you speak between donning arm and head tefillin, how many blessings?

✅ b) Two blessings — the original one plus a new blessing when donning the head tefillin.

2. Why no tefillin on Shabbat?

✅ b) Both tefillin and Shabbat are called "ot" (sign). Since Shabbat is already a sign, the additional sign of tefillin is unnecessary.

3. What does "guf naki" mean for tefillin?

✅ b) Guf naki means maintaining bodily cleanliness (not passing gas) and constant mindfulness of the tefillin while wearing them.

4. Already wearing tefillin at dusk — must you remove them?

✅ b) One who is already wearing tefillin at dusk need not remove them. But one should not put on new tefillin when it's uncertain whether it's still daytime.

5. Who holds that night IS a valid time for tefillin?

✅ b) R. Akiva holds night is a valid time for tefillin, though it's discouraged due to the risk of falling asleep.
