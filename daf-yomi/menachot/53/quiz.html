B. Like an olive tree whose purpose is fulfilled at its end, Israel's purpose will be fulfilled at their end
C. The merit of the Avot will protect them forever
D. Mashiach will come within the generation
Correct: B. A Bat Kol declared: "The Lord called your name a leafy olive tree." Just as an olive's purpose is fulfilled at its end (when fruit is picked), so too Israel will ultimately repent and return.
