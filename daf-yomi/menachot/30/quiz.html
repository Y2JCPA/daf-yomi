Menachot 30 — Quiz

1. How far can a tear extend and still be sewable?

✅ b) A tear through 2 lines can be sewn. Through 3+ lines, the sheet must be replaced.

2. What does "old parchment" mean in this context?

✅ b) "Old" = not processed with gall (gallnut solution). "New" = processed with gall. It's about the processing method, not age.

3. Who wrote the last 8 verses of the Torah according to R. Shimon?

✅ b) R. Shimon says Moses himself wrote all of it — God dictated and Moses wrote with tears (b'dema), even the verses describing his own death.

4. Where does halakha follow R. Shimon Shezuri?

✅ b) R. Chanina says halakha follows R. Shimon Shezuri wherever he teaches in the Mishna — but not in baraitot.

5. According to R. Meir, does oil form a connection between vessels for impurity?

✅ b) R. Meir says oil (thick) forms a connection but wine (thin) does not. R. Shimon Shezuri disagrees — even wine can form a connection.
