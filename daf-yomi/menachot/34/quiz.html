Menachot 34 — Quiz

1. What three letters spell God's Name on tefillin?

✅ b) The shin (ש) embossed on the box, the daled (ד) shaped knot on the head strap, and the yud (י) shaped knot on the arm strap together spell שדי (Shaddai).

2. Which passage is in the rightmost compartment?

✅ c) Kadesh (Exodus 13:1-10) goes in the rightmost compartment, following the order as they appear in the Torah.

3. What is the titora?

✅ b) The titora is the base of the tefillin upon which the compartments rest. It is halakha leMoshe miSinai.

4. Which side of the tefillin strap must face outward?

✅ b) The black side must face outward — this is halakha leMoshe miSinai.

5. How many prongs on each shin?

✅ b) One side has the regular 3-pronged shin; the other side has a unique 4-pronged shin. Both are halakha leMoshe miSinai.
