🌱 Replanted Produce — Core Question

Case: One separated tithes from produce, replanted it, and it grew additional produce. Do we follow the original (already tithed) component or the new growth (not yet tithed)?
Step 1: Original produce had Terumah/Ma'aser separated ✅

⚖️ Seed That Disintegrates vs. Seed That Doesn't

Seed Disintegrates

  • Original seed decays in soil
  • New growth is considered fresh produce
  • ✅ Clear: must separate new tithes

Seed Does NOT Disintegrate

  • Original kernel remains intact
  • New growth emerges while original persists
  • ❓ Safek: does old tithing cover this?
Rabba to Abaye: My question is specifically about seeds that do not disintegrate. If the seed disintegrates, everyone agrees the new growth needs fresh tithing.

🥖 Why Rice Is Excluded

Five Grains

  • Can become true chametz
  • Can be prevented from leavening → valid matzah
  • Included in Chalah/Chadash/Matzah

Rice/Millet

  • Do not become true chametz
  • They sour/decay (sirchon)
  • Excluded from these obligations
