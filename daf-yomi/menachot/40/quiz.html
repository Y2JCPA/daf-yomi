Menachot 40 — Quiz

1. Minimum garment size for tzitzit obligation?

✅ b) Must be large enough for a minor to cover head and most of the body.

2. Can you untie tzitzit from one garment and put them on another?

✅ b) May transfer tzitzit between garments as long as the strings are intact.

3. According to the Rabbis, must string color match the garment?

✅ b) The Rabbis hold any color string exempts any color garment.

4. How many corners must a garment have to be obligated?

✅ c) 4 or more corners. A garment with 5+ corners: put tzitzit on 4 of them. 3 corners: exempt.

5. "Exempted by strings of its own type" — "type" means:

✅ b) "Its own type" refers to the material — wool strings for wool garments, linen for linen.
