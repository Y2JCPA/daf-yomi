Chullin 53 Quiz

5 questions · Self-graded · Test your learning!
QUESTION 1 OF 5
According to Rav, what is the criterion for a spine-related treifa?
A. The majority of vertebral rings on one side must be shattered
B. The spinal cord must be severed through the majority of its width
C. The majority of ribs must be broken on both sides simultaneously
D. The animal must collapse and be unable to stand
Correct: B — Rav rules that the חוט השדרה (spinal cord) is the key structure — if it is severed through the majority of its width, the animal is treifa, regardless of whether the vertebrae themselves remain intact.
QUESTION 2 OF 5
What is the קרום של מוח, and what happens if it is perforated?
A. The tracheal lining — animal loses the ability to breathe normally
B. The lung pleura — animal requires a water test to confirm
C. The dura mater covering the brain — perforation renders the animal treifa with no dispute
D. The spinal canal membrane — animal is only treifa if the cord is also severed
Correct: C — קרום של מוח is the dura mater, the membrane enveloping the brain. Its perforation is one of the classic 18 treifot in the Mishnah — universally agreed to render the animal treifa, regardless of other conditions.
QUESTION 3 OF 5
What did Rav Huna rule regarding broken ribs?
A. Even a single broken rib near the heart renders the animal treifa
B. Rib injuries are only relevant if the spine is also broken
C. If the majority of ribs on one side are broken, the animal is treifa
D. Broken ribs create only a rabbinic doubt requiring a stringent ruling
Correct: C — Rav Huna rules נשברו רוב הצלעות — if the majority of ribs on one side are broken, the thoracic cavity can no longer protect and support the vital organs, and the animal is treifa.
QUESTION 4 OF 5
What is the practical method used to detect a perforation in the lung?
A. Slice the lung open lengthwise and examine for internal discoloration
B. Inflate the lung and submerge it in water — bubbles indicate a hole
C. Weigh the lung and compare it to a healthy baseline
D. Press on the lung surface and listen for a crackling sound
Correct: B — The classic בדיקת ריאה involves inflating the lung and submerging it in water. Air bubbles rising from the submerged lung confirm a perforation (ניקב), rendering the animal treifa.
QUESTION 5 OF 5
What is Shmuel's criterion for when a broken spine renders an animal treifa?
A. The spinal cord must be entirely severed, not just majority
B. The majority of vertebral rings (חוליות) on one side must be broken
C. Both the cord and vertebrae must be damaged simultaneously
D. The animal must be unable to bear weight on its hind legs
Correct: B — Shmuel focuses on the structural integrity of the vertebral column itself: if the majority of the חוליות (vertebral rings) are broken on one side, the animal is treifa — the cord's condition is secondary to Shmuel.
