⚖️ Rav vs. Shmuel: Spine Treifa Criteria

Rav
The חוט השדרה (spinal cord) itself is the key: if it is severed through the majority of its width, the animal is treifa — even if the vertebrae remain intact.
Shmuel
The שדרה (vertebral column) is determinative: if the majority of חוליות (vertebral rings) on one side are broken, the animal is treifa — regardless of cord condition.
Rav Huna
Even when cord and vertebrae are intact — if the majority of צלעות (ribs) on one side are broken, the animal cannot survive and is treifa.
Gemara
All opinions converge on the underlying rule: any injury from which the animal אינה יכולה לחיות — cannot survive twelve months — renders it treifa.
Core Principle Treifa = any condition incompatible with twelve-month survival. Spine, cord, and rib rulings are derived applications of this standard.

🦴 Three Spine-Area Treifot Compared

Severed Spinal Cord

  • חוט השדרה שנפסק
  • Majority of cord width cut
  • Rav's criterion: focus on cord
  • Vertebrae may be intact
  • → Treifa

Broken Vertebral Column

  • שדרה שנשברה
  • Majority of vertebral rings broken
  • Shmuel: structural integrity key
  • One side sufficient
  • → Treifa

Majority Ribs Broken

  • נשברו רוב הצלעות
  • Rav Huna's ruling
  • Rib cage can't support organs
  • One side, cord/spine intact
  • → Treifa

💀 Puncture of the Dura Mater

ניקב קרום של מוח — הרי זו טריפה (משנה חולין מב.)
ניקב קרום של מוח — Treifa, No Dispute If the קרום של מוח (dura mater — the membrane enveloping the brain) is perforated, the animal is definitively treifa. This is among the eighteen classic treifot of the Mishnah.

The Gemara clarifies: a crack in the skull alone (סדק בגולגולת) does not suffice — only an actual breach of the membrane renders the animal treifa. The skull provides protection; the membrane is the vital barrier.

🫁 Lung Examination Procedure (בדיקת ריאה)

Step 1 — Visual Survey: Inspect the exterior for discoloration, lesions, or visibly missing lobes (חסרון אונות). Any removed lobe = treifa immediately.
↓
Step 2 — Check Adhesions (סירכות): Note any adhesions between lobes or between lung and chest wall. Their direction and texture determine whether they are kosher or treifa.
↓
Step 3 — Water Test: Inflate the lung and submerge in water. Rising air bubbles confirm a perforation (ניקב) — the animal is treifa.
↓
Step 4 — Evaluate Adhesion Type: Lengthwise (lobe-to-lobe) adhesions that peel cleanly may be kosher; adhering across natural boundaries is more stringent.
↓
Step 5 — Final Ruling: No hole, no removed lobe, and permissible adhesions → כשרה (kosher). Any perforation or lobe loss → טריפה.
