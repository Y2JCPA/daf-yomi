🏛️ The Sanctity of Beit She'an

The setup: Rabbi Yehuda HaNasi exempted Beit She'an from terumot and tithes. But wait — isn't Beit She'an in Eretz Yisrael? How can it be exempt?
The answer: Many cities conquered by the olei Mitzrayim (returnees from Egypt under Joshua) were not reconquered by the olei Bavel (returnees from Babylon under Ezra). Rebbi held that the first sanctification was only temporary — and these cities were intentionally left unsanctified so the poor could eat from them during Shemittah.
הַרְבֵּה כְּרַכִּים כְּבָשׁוּם עוֹלֵי מִצְרַיִם וְלֹא כְבָשׁוּם עוֹלֵי בָבֶל

"Many cities were conquered by those who left Egypt but not by those who left Babylon"

🍃 The Leaf of R' Meir

RY
R' Meir once ate a leaf in Beit She'an without tithing it. Based on this, Rebbi exempted the whole city!
RI
Maybe he just wasn't paying attention when he ate it?
RZ
If Hashem doesn't bring mishap even through the animals of the righteous, certainly not the righteous themselves!
RI
Maybe he tithed that leaf from produce elsewhere?
RZ
Chaverim are not suspected of tithing from produce that is not adjacent. R' Meir intentionally ate it untithed — proving Beit She'an was exempt!

RY = R' Yirmeya · RI = R' Yirmeya's challenges · RZ = R' Zeira

🌊 R' Pinchas ben Ya'ir & the Ginai River

R' Pinchas ben Ya'ir travels to redeem captives (pidyon shvuyim) 🏃
↓
The Ginai River blocks his path — too deep to cross
↓
"Ginai! Split your waters for me!"
↓
River argues: "You might do God's will; I definitely am (flowing as commanded)!"
↓
"If you don't split — I'll decree you never carry water again!"
↓
The river splits! ✨ THREE times — once for him, once for a man carrying Pesach wheat, and once for an Arab traveler
Rav Yosef marveled: "How great is this man! Greater than Moshe and 600,000 — for there, the sea split once. Here, the river split three times!"

🫏 The Donkey That Wouldn't Eat

1️⃣

Barley Offered

At an inn, hosts place barley before R' Pinchas ben Ya'ir's donkey. The donkey refuses to eat.

2️⃣

Sifted & Cleaned

They sift the barley, clean it by hand, remove chaff — still the donkey refuses.

3️⃣

Tithes Separated

R' Pinchas says: "Perhaps it hasn't been tithed?" They tithe the barley — and the donkey eats!

🎓 The principle: "God does not bring mishap even through the animals of the righteous" — בְּהֶמְתָּן שֶׁל צַדִּיקִים אֵין הַקָּדוֹשׁ בָּרוּךְ הוּא מֵבִיא תַּקָּלָה עַל יָדָן

🐴 Rebbi's White Mules

Rebbi Invites

  • 🍽️ R' Pinchas arrives; Rebbi goes out to greet him
  • 💬 "Will you dine with me?"
  • ✅ R' Pinchas agrees — he's not against benefit from Jews
  • ⏳ But first: "I'm rushing for a mitzvah — I'll come back"

The White Mules

  • 🐴 When he returns, R' Pinchas enters through a gate where white mules stand
  • 💀 "The Angel of Death is in this house! Shall I dine here?!"
  • 💰 Rebbi: "I'll sell them!" — "You'll cause others to stumble"
  • 🚫 "Abandon them?" — "You'll increase the damage"
  • ⛰️ A mountain rose between them — they could not reconcile

White mules were considered dangerous — bred from mixed species and known for kicking
