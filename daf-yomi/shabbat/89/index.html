👑 Moses Finds God Tying Crowns to the Letters

Moses ascends on High and finds the Holy One, Blessed be He, tying crowns (tagim) — ornamental crownlets — onto certain letters of the Torah
↓
Moses stands silently. God asks: "Moses, is there no greeting of peace in your city?"
↓
Moses: "Does a servant greet his master first?" — God: "You should have assisted Me nonetheless!"
↓
Moses asks: "Why are You adding these crowns?" God: "There will arise a man — Akiva ben Yosef — who will derive mounds and mounds of laws from each and every crown"
↓
Moses: "Master of the Universe, show him to me." God transports Moses to Rabbi Akiva's study hall, where Moses sits in the eighth row
↓
Moses cannot follow the discussion and his strength wanes — until a student asks: "From where do you know this?" and Akiva replies: "It is a law given to Moses at Sinai." Moses is consoled.

🏔️ Why Is the Mountain Called Sinai? — Multiple Explanations

Reasons for the Name 'Sinai'

  • Nissim (נִסִּים) — miracles were performed for Israel upon it
  • Sinah (שִׂנְאָה) — hatred descended upon the nations of the world there, when Israel received the Torah
  • Paru v'ravu (פָּרוּ וְרָבוּ) — Israel was fruitful and multiplied in the Sinai Desert
  • True name: Horeb (חֹרֵב) — the primary name used in other biblical contexts

"Ba'u Shesh" — The People's Fatal Miscalculation

  • Verse: "The people saw that Moses boshesh (delayed)" (Exodus 32:1)
  • Read not boshesh but ba'u shesh — six hours had come
  • Satan showed the people an image of Moses' bier carried through the sky
  • People concluded Moses had died → panic → Golden Calf
