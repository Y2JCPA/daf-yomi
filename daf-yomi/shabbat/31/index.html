🤝 Three Partners in the Creation of a Person

The Father contributes white seed → bones, sinews, nails, brain, whites of eyes
↓
The Mother contributes red seed → skin, flesh, blood, hair, pupils of eyes
↓
The Holy One, Blessed be He contributes → soul, facial expression, sight, hearing, speech, motion, and understanding
↓
When a person departs from the world, God retrieves His part and leaves the father's and mother's portions before them — as the adage goes: Remove what is yours from my property

💬 When Does Conception Most Likely Occur?

Rabbi Yitzḥak / Rabbi Ami
A woman conceives only near the onset of her menstrual cycle: "Behold, I was brought forth in iniquity" (Ps. 51:7) — the 'iniquity' is intercourse at the time of menstrual onset
Rabbi Yoḥanan
A woman conceives only near the time of her immersion (purification from niddah): from the same verse — "and in sin [ḥet] did my mother conceive me" — ḥet denotes purity, as in veḥittei the house (Lev. 14:52)
Practical Convergence Both teachings direct intercourse toward the window near the wife's immersion — the primary permitted time after her niddah period ends

🔍 Four Questions, Four Answers

תלמידי רבי דוסתאי בר' ינאי
Rabbi Dostai bar Yannai — On the Differences Between Men and Women Why does man pursue woman? Like one who lost an object — the loser seeks what was lost. Adam was formed from earth; woman was taken from man. Each seeks their origin.

Why does man face down, woman face up in intercourse? Man faces his origin (the earth below); woman faces her creator (man).

Why does man accept appeasement but woman does not? Man acts like earth — easily appeased when struck; woman acts like a rib — harder to move once it takes its shape.

Why is a woman's voice pleasant, not a man's? Man is like earth — earth makes no sound when struck with a stick; woman is like a rib — when struck, a rib produces sound.
