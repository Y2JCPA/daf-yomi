⚖️ Who May Dissolve Mustard on Shabbat?

Rav
Vessel permitted, hand forbidden — using a vessel diverges from normal weekday method, making it sufficiently unusual for Shabbat
Shmuel
Hand is fine — dissolving by hand every day would make it 'donkey food'; the hand is not the normal method, so no issue
R. Elazar (E.Y.)
Both prohibited — neither hand nor vessel may be used to dissolve mustard on Shabbat
R. Yoḥanan (E.Y.)
Both permitted — no restriction on either method of dissolving mustard on Shabbat
Mar Zutra
Final ruling: both permitted — halakha is not per any of the disputed opinions above; dissolve freely by hand or vessel
Why the confusion? Abaye and Rava were left uncertain because R. Elazar and R. Yoḥanan each reversed positions mid-debate — until Mar Zutra settled the matter with a clean lenient ruling.

🌶️ The Mustard Dissolution Debate: Shifting Positions

Rav
Vessel ✓ / Hand ✗
Using a vessel diverges from the normal daily method — that divergence is what renders it Shabbat-appropriate
Shmuel
Hand ✓ / Vessel ?
'If you dissolve it by hand every day, that's donkey food!' — hand is clearly not the normal method, so no concern
R. Elazar
Both ✗
Initially: both hand and vessel are forbidden on Shabbat — later switched to Shmuel's position
R. Yoḥanan
Both ✓
Initially: both methods are permitted — later switched to R. Elazar's strict position
Mar Zutra
Final psak: Both ✓
The halakha follows neither Rav nor R. Elazar — one may dissolve by hand or vessel freely
Lived Reality Abaye's foster mother and Ze'eira's wife both prepared mustard — but their students declined to eat it, unsure of the ruling. The uncertainty was real and practical.

🥤 Anumlin vs. Aluntit — Two Drinks, Two Rulings

✅ Anumlin — Permitted on Shabbat

  • Composition: Wine + honey + pepper
  • Classified as a normal drinking beverage
  • May be prepared fresh on Shabbat itself
  • Rav Yosef: 'One cup chills me from head-hairs to toenails — a second cup could have neutralized me entirely'
  • Its pleasure and food-like nature exempt it from the medicine prohibition

❌ Aluntit — Forbidden on Shabbat

  • Composition: Old wine + clear water + balsam
  • Classified as a therapeutic/medicinal preparation
  • The intent to heal (refuah) triggers the Shabbat medicine prohibition
  • Preparing it on Shabbat constitutes a rabbinic violation
  • Even if it could be drunk for pleasure, the medicinal framing is decisive

💎 Rav Ḥisda's Teaching to His Daughters: The Pearl and the Clod

מסכת שבת קמ ע״ב — עצת רב חסדא לבנותיו
Modesty as Strategy Dietary counsel: Don't eat bread before your husband (you'll eat too much and be demeaned); avoid vegetables at night (bad breath); avoid dates and beer at night (they loosen the bowels); don't relieve yourself in the place where your husband does.

Speech: When someone knocks, answer in the feminine — avoid any impression of dealings with other men.

The Pearl Demonstration: Rav Ḥisda held a pearl in one hand and a clod of earth in the other. He showed the pearl immediately — but withheld the clod until his daughters were consumed with curiosity.

The lesson: What is modest and hidden is more precious and desired than what is freely displayed. Modesty generates value; exposure diminishes it.
