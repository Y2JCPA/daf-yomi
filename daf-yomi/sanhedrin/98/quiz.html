Sanhedrin 98 Quiz

5 questions · Self-graded · Test your learning!
QUESTION 1 OF 5
According to Rabbi Abba, what is the most explicit sign that the end of days is near?
A. A fish cannot be found for the sick
B. The mountains of Israel give forth their fruit to the returning Jewish people
C. Rome's gate falls for the third time
D. All judges and officers cease from Israel
Correct: B — Rabbi Abba explicitly states that no sign is clearer than the mountains of Israel yielding their fruit to the Jewish people, citing Ezekiel 36:8 — the Land's productivity for its returning people heralds the redemption.
QUESTION 2 OF 5
When Rabbi Yehoshua ben Levi met the Messiah at Rome's gate and asked 'When will you come?', what did the Messiah answer?
A. When all of Israel repents
B. When the Roman kingdom falls
C. Today — if you heed His voice (Psalm 95:7)
D. After seven years of tribulation
Correct: C — The Messiah said 'Today!' — which puzzled R. Yehoshua ben Levi since he didn't come. Elijah explained: the Messiah meant 'today — if you heed His voice' (Psalm 95:7), making redemption contingent on that very day's response.
QUESTION 3 OF 5
Why did Rabba say 'Let the Messiah come but after my death' — i.e., why wasn't he reassured by the baraita saying Torah study protects from Messianic suffering?
A. He didn't believe in Torah's protective power
B. He feared sin might cause the protection to be forfeited, per R. Ya'akov bar Idi
C. He was concerned about the Persians, not the Messiah
D. He thought the baraita only applied to Tannaim, not Amoraim
Correct: B — Rabba acknowledged the baraita's principle but feared his own sins might invalidate the promise for him personally. R. Ya'akov bar Idi demonstrated from Jacob's experience that divine assurances can be suspended by transgression.
QUESTION 4 OF 5
According to Rav Yehuda citing Rav, how does the Messiah relate to David — will it literally be David or someone new?
A. The Messiah will be a reincarnation of the soul of David
B. God will establish a new David — a different king called by David's name
C. David himself will be resurrected to reign as Messiah
D. The name 'David' in the verse refers to God, not a human king
Correct: B — Rav Yehuda citing Rav teaches that God will establish 'another David' — a new king named David. The proof is from Jeremiah 30:9: 'David their king whom I will establish for them' — the future tense 'establish' implies a new appointment.
QUESTION 5 OF 5
What sign did Rabbi Yosei ben Kisma give his students to verify his prophecy about Rome's gate — and did it come true?
A. The sun would darken for three days — and it did
B. The Jordan River would reverse its flow — it did not
C. The water of the Cave of Pamyas would turn to blood — and it did
D. A great earthquake would strike Rome — and it occurred
Correct: C — R. Yosei ben Kisma initially refused to give a sign but relented: 'The water of the Cave of Pamyas will be transformed into blood.' The Gemara confirms: 'And it was transformed into blood' — validating his prophetic credibility.
