🔮 Daniel vs. The Three Prophets

Haggai, Zechariah & Malachi

  • Greater: they held full prophetic status
  • Transmitted divine prophecy to the people
  • Did not see the terrifying vision directly
  • Yet their guardian angels saw it and fled in fear
  • Inexplicable terror — mediated by angelic witness

Daniel

  • Greater: he personally saw the vision himself
  • Directly witnessed the terrifying heavenly apparition
  • He saw — yet the others were frightened without seeing
  • Remedy for unexplained dread: leap 4 cubits OR recite Shema
  • His visionary experience was more vivid and direct

⚔️ Was Sennacherib Clever or Foolish?

Rav
He was a CLEVER king. He told the exiles: 'I will take you to a land like your own.' He chose his words carefully — making the exile sound acceptable and reducing resistance from the population.
Shmuel
He was a FOOLISH king. He said 'a land like your own' — which implies their current land is actually better! This would inflame their attachment to Israel and increase resistance, not reduce it.
Rabbi Yoḥanan
Why was Sennacherib called 'the great and noble Asenappar'? Because he did not speak disparagingly of Eretz Yisrael — saying only 'a land like your own land, a land of grain and wine' (Isaiah 36:17).
Principle Both opinions agree Sennacherib was strategic. The dispute: did 'like your own land' cleverly neutralize resistance, or did it backfire by implicitly praising Israel?

📚 Hezekiah's Radical Torah Mandate

כתב חרב על פתח בית המדרש ואמר כל מי שאינו עוסק בתורה ידקר בחרב זו
החרב על פתח בית המדרש Hezekiah inserted a sword at the entrance of the study hall and declared:

"Anyone who does not engage in Torah study shall be stabbed with this sword."

The result was unprecedented: they searched from Dan to Beersheba and found no ignoramus. From Gabbath to Antiparis — no child or woman who was not expert in the laws of ritual purity and impurity.

The prophet testified of that generation (Isaiah 7:22): "Butter and honey shall every one eat that is left in the land" — only Torah scholars remained in the land.
