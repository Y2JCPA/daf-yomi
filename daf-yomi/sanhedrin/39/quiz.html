Sanhedrin 39 Quiz

5 questions · Self-graded · Test your learning!
QUESTION 1 OF 5
How did Rabban Gamliel's daughter counter the emperor's claim that God was a thief for taking Adam's rib?
A. She quoted Genesis to show Adam voluntarily gave his rib
B. She said a thief who leaves something more valuable than he took is actually a benefactor
C. She argued that Adam was not harmed because he woke up whole
D. She said God had permission from Adam before putting him to sleep
Correct: B — She told the emperor: imagine thieves who take a silver vessel but leave a gold one — you would welcome them nightly! God took one rib but gave Adam a wife — a far greater gift.
QUESTION 2 OF 5
Why did God forbid the angels from singing when the Egyptians drowned at the Red Sea?
A. The Song of the Sea had not yet been taught to the angels
B. Only Moses and Israel had permission to praise God at that moment
C. God said: My creations are drowning and you wish to sing songs before Me?
D. The angels were occupied with guiding the pillars of cloud and fire
Correct: C — God's rebuke teaches a core principle: He does not rejoice at the downfall of the wicked, even the wicked Egyptians — because they too are His creations.
QUESTION 3 OF 5
According to Rabbi Yitzḥak, why did Obadiah merit receiving prophecy?
A. He publicly rebuked Ahab and Jezebel in their own palace
B. He descended from a long line of prophets in the tribe of Ephraim
C. He concealed one hundred prophets in a cave and sustained them when Jezebel sought to kill them
D. He fasted and prayed in the wilderness for forty days
Correct: C — Rabbi Yitzḥak says Obadiah merited prophecy because he hid one hundred prophets in a cave (fifty per cave) and provided them with bread and water (I Kings 18:4).
QUESTION 4 OF 5
How did Rabban Gamliel refute the claim that two gods created the universe — one using the verb 'forms' (mountains) and another using 'creates' (wind) in Amos 4:13?
A. He showed that the same verb is used for both in the book of Psalms
B. He pointed out that a single human face contains two different types of orifices, yet was made by one Creator
C. He argued that both verbs appear in Genesis 1 as synonyms
D. He demonstrated that in Aramaic the two verbs are identical
Correct: B — Rabban Gamliel argued: would you say two separate gods created the eyes and ears of a single human face? Of course not. Diversity in creation reflects the infinite creativity of one God, not multiple gods.
QUESTION 5 OF 5
When the heretic asked what mikveh God used before burying Moses (since God commanded teruma, implying priestly status), how did Rabbi Abbahu respond?
A. God was exempt from immersion because He never contracts impurity
B. He immersed in the Jordan River, which has special sanctity
C. He immersed in fire — 'For, behold, the Lord will come in fire' (Isaiah 66:15)
D. He immersed in the waters above the firmament created on the second day
Correct: C — Rabbi Abbahu said God immersed in fire (Isaiah 66:15), then added that fire is actually the primary form of purification — as the Torah itself states: 'everything that can withstand fire, you shall pass through fire' (Numbers 31:23).
