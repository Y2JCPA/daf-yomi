Sanhedrin 45 Quiz

5 questions · Self-graded · Test your learning!
QUESTION 1 OF 5
According to the Rabbis, why is a condemned woman covered with two pieces of cloth during stoning while a man is covered with only one?
A. Women have more sensitive skin and require greater physical protection
B. To show that women are judged more leniently than men
C. Out of concern that seeing a naked woman would arouse improper thoughts among the witnesses
D. Because women's clothing is more easily removed and two pieces ensure coverage
Correct: C — The Rabbis derive from the verse 'stone him' that a man is stoned with exposure; but a woman is not, because the Rabbis are concerned that seeing her naked would arouse sexual thoughts among the witnesses standing by.
QUESTION 2 OF 5
How does Rava resolve the apparent contradiction in the Rabbis' position — humiliating the sota publicly, yet refusing to stone a woman naked?
A. The sota procedure is rabbinic, while stoning is biblical — different standards apply
B. The sota's public exposure serves a deterrent purpose (Ezek. 23:48); adding humiliation to execution would impose two punishments, which 'love your neighbor' forbids
C. The Rabbis changed their opinion between the sota ruling and the stoning ruling
D. The sota is humiliated by the priest alone privately, not in front of a crowd
Correct: B — Rava explains: the sota's disgrace deters other women from lewdness (Ezekiel 23:48). That purpose justifies the humiliation. But a condemned woman already receives death — stripping her would pile on a second punishment, violating 'love your neighbor as yourself.'
QUESTION 3 OF 5
What is the total effective height of the fall at a stoning execution, and why?
A. One body-height — just enough to cause injury
B. Two body-heights — the platform height alone
C. Three body-heights — platform (×2) plus the condemned person's own height
D. Four body-heights — to guarantee death without any additional stoning
Correct: C — The Gemara teaches: the platform is twice the height of a person, and the condemned man stands on top. So the total height of the fall is three times a person's height — ensuring a swift, lethal fall in accordance with 'love your neighbor as yourself.'
QUESTION 4 OF 5
According to Shmuel, what is the law if the witnesses who testified against a condemned man have their hands severed AFTER the sentence is issued?
A. The court may appoint other witnesses to cast the first stones
B. The transgressor is executed without the witnesses' participation
C. The transgressor is exempt from execution
D. The sentence is suspended until the witnesses heal
Correct: C — Shmuel rules the transgressor is exempt. The verse (Deut. 17:7) states 'the hand of the witnesses shall be first upon him' — the witnesses themselves must physically cast the first stone. If they cannot, the execution cannot be carried out.
QUESTION 5 OF 5
According to the Rabbis (opposing Rabbi Eliezer), which corpses are hung after execution by stoning, and why specifically those?
A. All those executed by stoning, because the verse about hanging appears in the stoning section
B. Only murderers and those who committed violent crimes
C. Only the blasphemer and the idol worshipper, because they denied the fundamental principle of belief in God
D. No one — the Rabbis hold that hanging corpses is entirely prohibited
Correct: C — The Rabbis hold that just as the blasphemer (who cursed God) denied the fundamental principle, so too only idol worshippers — who also deny the fundamental — are hung after death. Others, even if liable to stoning, are not hung, because hanging is a mark for denying God specifically.
