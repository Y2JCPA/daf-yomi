🌙 Disputes Over the Moon Blessing

Rav Ya'akov bar Idi
One may bless the new moon only until the 7th of the month — while the moon is still visibly waxing in its first half.
Rav Aḥa of Difti
One may bless until the 16th of the month — until the moon reaches complete fullness and its 'flaw' is entirely filled.
Rav Aḥa of Difti (query to Ravina)
Should one also recite HaTov veHaMeitiv — 'Who is good and Who does good' — for the benefit people receive from the moon's light?
Ravina
HaTov veHaMeitiv requires two people to jointly benefit from a shared new good. The moon benefits all people equally and generally — this blessing does not apply here.
Gemara ruling
The correct blessing is Rav Yehuda's full formulation — not the abbreviated 'Who renews the months' used in the West.
📜 Full Blessing (Rav Yehuda) Blessed are You… Who by His word created the heavens and by the breath of His mouth all their hosts. He set for them a law and a time that they should not alter their task. They are joyous and glad to perform the will of their Creator…

🤲 Blessing the Moon = Greeting the Shechina

כָּל הַמְבָרֵךְ עַל הַחֹדֶשׁ בִּזְמַנּוֹ כְּאִילּוּ מְקַבֵּל פְּנֵי שְׁכִינָה
Rabbi Yoḥanan (via Rabbi Asi via Rabbi Aḥa bar Ḥanina) Anyone who blesses the new month in its proper time, it is as if he greets the Face of the Divine Presence. The verse describing the moon's renewal uses the same root ל-ק-ר-א (to go toward / to greet) as the verse about encountering God — creating a verbal parallel between the two acts. Additionally: the verse 'For by wise advice you shall make your war' (Prov. 24:6) teaches that Torah scholars who engage in Torah study are as if they wage the wars of the House of David.

⚖️ 'The Judgment Has Grown Aged' — Two Types of Cases

💰 Monetary Cases

  • A judge MAY announce: 'The judgment has grown aged' (נִבְלַע הַדִּין)
  • Signals: the case is complex / deadlocked / requires scrutiny
  • The greatest judge among them states this announcement
  • Being shamed by oneself is preferable to being shamed by others — so the leader speaks first
  • After announcing, the court proceeds to resolve or refer

⚔️ Capital Cases

  • One does NOT say 'The judgment has grown aged'
  • If deadlocked, judges are added two at a time
  • Court expands up to 71 (full Sanhedrin HaGadol)
  • If still deadlocked at 71 (36–35), the defendant is released
  • Every avenue is exhausted — human life cannot be surrendered to impasse
