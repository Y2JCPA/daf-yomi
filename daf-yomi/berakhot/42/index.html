🏛️ Encapsulated vs. Concealed: The Central Body-Space Dispute

Abaye
The vagina is encapsulated (בלוע). Because it has an opening, impurity inside it renders a person impure just as if it were outside.
Rava
The vagina is a concealed part (בית הסתרים). Proof: the Torah had to write a special novelty teaching that semen inside renders impure — meaning it would not normally do so.
Abaye
Rabbi Shimon's statement is a double novelty: (1) semen inside the body imparts impurity, AND (2) it does so even in a concealed space. No proof for concealed-only classification.
Rava
Same logic applies to the throat: a bird carcass there renders impure only through swallowing — unique to that space, confirming it is concealed, not merely encapsulated.
Practical Difference If a woman inserts an olive-bulk of animal carcass into another woman's vagina — Abaye: she is impure. Rava: she is pure, since the vagina is a concealed space.

💬 Rav Shmuel bar Bisna's Dilemma — Abaye & Rava Respond

Rav Shmuel bar Bisna
Question to Abaye: A woman who discharges semen — is she a source of emission (מקור טומאה) or merely one who came into contact with impurity (נוגעת)?
Abaye
She has the status of one who experienced an emission — treated like a man who emitted semen.
Rava
Same ruling as Abaye: she is a source of emission. Rav Shmuel bar Bisna came and asked me the same question and I agreed.
Abaye
Clarification: Rabbi Shimon's leniency — 'it is sufficient for her to be like the man' — applies only to immersion timing, not to negate her emission status or her seven clean days.
Why Ask at All? The Gemara's Difficulty If he knew the baraita → the Rabbis' answer is obvious. If he didn't → contact-status seems obvious. Resolution: he asked specifically according to Rabbi Shimon's opinion, where the question is genuine.

⚗️ Emission Status vs. Contact Status — Three Practical Differences

מקור טומאה — Source of Emission

  • Negates her seven clean days (שבעה נקיים)
  • Any amount (כל שהוא) renders her impure
  • Renders impure through carrying (משא)
  • Treated like a man who emitted semen
  • ✅ Ruling of both Abaye and Rava

נוגעת בטומאה — Contact with Impurity

  • Does NOT negate her seven clean days
  • Only olive-bulk (כזית) renders impure
  • Does NOT render impure through carrying
  • Treated as secondary contact only
  • ❌ Rejected by both Abaye and Rava

🤰 Woman After Childbirth Descending to Immerse

Woman after childbirth descends to immerse in mikveh
Days of impurity have passed; she seeks purification
↓
Blood appears on descent
Impure!
Sages rendered this blood like an unslaughtered bird carcass — imparts impurity even while still inside the body
Blood appears on ascent
Pure!
Blood outside the body renders impure only through contact — she was already pure when she immersed
↓
Why impure on descent? — Rabbi Yirmeya objects: an encapsulated impurity cannot render impure — why is she impure?
↓
Rabbi Avin answers: Rabbinic decree — like the unslaughtered bird carcass (נבלת עוף טהור), which imparts impurity even while enclosed inside one's body, a unique Torah novelty

🐛 Rava's Three Rulings on Body Folds

Case 1: A creeping animal (שרץ) is found in the fold of one's body (e.g., armpit)
↓
➔ Pure! A creeping animal imparts impurity through contact — a concealed body fold is not susceptible to contact-type impurity
↓
Case 2: Animal carcass flesh (נבלה) is found in the fold of one's body
↓
➔ Impure! Animal carcass imparts impurity through carrying (משא), not just contact — the body carries it even in a fold
↓
Case 3: A creeping animal in the fold is brought into the airspace of an oven
↓
➔ Oven is impure! The person's body functions like a vessel, carrying the creeping animal into the oven's airspace and rendering it impure
