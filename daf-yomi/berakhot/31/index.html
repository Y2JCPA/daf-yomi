🤝 Three Partners in the Creation of a Person

The Holy One

  • The soul
  • Expression of the face
  • Eyesight and hearing
  • Speech and the ability to walk
  • Sense and understanding
  • God retrieves His part at death

The Father

  • White seed
  • Bones and sinews
  • Nails
  • Brain matter
  • White of the eye
  • Father's part remains after death

The Mother

  • Red seed
  • Skin and flesh
  • Hair
  • Pupil of the eye
  • Mother's part remains after death

📍 Fetus Position Through the Three Trimesters

Months 1–3: Fetus resides in the lower compartment of the womb.
Source: "I was woven together in the lowest parts of the earth" (Ps. 139:15) — R. Elazar
↓
Months 4–6: Fetus moves to the middle compartment of the womb.
Intercourse is difficult for the woman but beneficial for the offspring during this period
↓
Months 7–9: Fetus ascends to the upper compartment of the womb.
Intercourse on the 90th day is like spilling her blood — the fetus is nearly fully formed
↓
At birth: The fetus turns upside down — this reversal causes labor pains.
↓
Why female birth is more painful: A female fetus faces upward and must rotate further to exit face-down; a male fetus already faces down toward his place of creation.

⚧️ Determining the Sex of a Fetus

Intercourse occurs — whose seed is emitted first?
↓
Woman emits seed first
Child will be Male
"If a woman emits seed and gives birth to a male" (Lev. 12:2)
Man emits seed first
Child will be Female
The male seed arrives later, yielding a daughter
↓
Practical application: The sons of Ulam (I Chr. 8:40) delayed their own emission so wives would emit first — Scripture credits them as if they had many sons and sons' sons

🙋 Why Does a New Mother Bring an Offering?

Students
For what reason does the Torah require a woman after childbirth to bring an offering?
R. Shimon ben Yoḥai
At the moment she crouches in agony to give birth, the pain is so great that she impulsively vows: 'I will never have intercourse with my husband again!' The Torah requires her to bring an offering to dissolve this rash oath.
Rav Yosef
Objection! When she later resumes intercourse she becomes an intentional violator of her oath. Dissolving an oath requires genuine regret — can the offering alone accomplish this?
Resolution
The act of bringing the offering itself constitutes and expresses her regret. Both the regret and the atonement are present in the offering — the two are combined in the single act.
R. Shimon ben Yoḥai (also)
Why is a woman who gives birth to a female impure 14 days rather than 7? Because after a female birth, even the mother is not overjoyed — so she takes a full 14 days to become fully consoled and happy.
📖 Source Leviticus 12:6 — on the completion of her purification days, she shall bring a lamb for a burnt-offering and a pigeon or turtledove for a sin-offering

🌟 God's Attribute vs. Human Attribute

שְׁלֹשָׁה שֻׁתָּפִין בָּאָדָם — הַקָּדוֹשׁ בָּרוּךְ הוּא, וְאָבִיו, וְאִמּוֹ (נידה לא.)
אגדה — Aggadic Wonders of Creation R. Ḥinnana bar Pappa (Job 9:10): A human dyer places many herbs in a cauldron — they all emerge the same color. But the Holy One forms the fetus from a single drop, and each seed emerges as its own distinct type — bone, flesh, sinew, etc.

R. Yosei HaGelili (Ps. 139:14): "I am fearfully and wonderfully made" — God stamps every person with the same seal (Adam's), yet not one person is identical to another.

R. Ḥanina bar Pappa (Ps. 139:3): A person is not created from the entire drop but only from its most refined part — as it is written: "You measure my going about and my lying down" — zerita refers to the best select portion.
