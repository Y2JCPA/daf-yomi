Shekalim 15 Quiz

5 questions · Self-graded · Test your learning!
QUESTION 1 OF 5
Why did the House of Avtinas refuse to teach others their incense formula?
A. They wanted to earn more money from the Temple
B. They feared the formula would be used for idol worship outside the Temple
C. The formula was lost and they were ashamed to admit it
D. The Kohen Gadol commanded them to keep it secret
Correct: B — The Gemara records their defense: they feared that if others learned the technique, it might be misused for idol worship. Despite this, the Sages still criticized them for withholding sacred knowledge.
QUESTION 2 OF 5
How did the women of the House of Avtinas demonstrate their personal integrity?
A. They wore plain white garments whenever entering the Temple
B. They donated their jewelry to the Temple treasury annually
C. They never wore perfume, so none could suspect them of diverting Temple incense
D. They fasted on every Rosh Chodesh as an act of atonement
Correct: C — To demonstrate they did not personally benefit from the Temple incense, no woman of the House of Avtinas wore perfume — a powerful demonstration of personal scrupulousness.
QUESTION 3 OF 5
What was Hugras ben Levi's special vocal technique for Temple song?
A. He would inhale deeply and hold his breath for an extended trill
B. He placed his thumb in his mouth to produce an extraordinary musical trill
C. He sang while bowing prostrate on the Temple floor
D. He used a special reed instrument hidden in his sleeve
Correct: B — Hugras ben Levi would place his thumb in his mouth to produce a remarkable trill (no'imah). Like the other craftsmen, he refused to teach this technique to others and was criticized for it.
QUESTION 4 OF 5
What role did Elazar serve among the fifteen Temple officers?
A. Over the shewbread preparation
B. Over the priestly lottery system
C. Over the Temple curtains (parochet)
D. Over the daily incense offering
Correct: C — Elazar was the officer appointed over the curtains (פרוכת) — responsible for their weaving, maintenance, purification, and storage. This role was critical given the extreme sanctity of the parochet.
QUESTION 5 OF 5
What is the Sages' general critique of the House of Garmu and House of Avtinas?
A. They charged excessive wages from the Temple treasury
B. They performed their duties improperly and negligently
C. They withheld sacred craft knowledge that should have been shared with all Israel
D. They intermarried with non-priestly families against Temple law
Correct: C — Although both families acted from arguably protective motives and demonstrated personal integrity, the Sages criticized them for keeping their skills as hereditary secrets. Sacred knowledge belongs to the entire community of Israel.
