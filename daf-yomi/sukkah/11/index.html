✂️ Is Cutting Enough? — The Central Dispute of Daf 11

Rav
If climbing plants (grapevine, gourd, ivy) are used as schach while still attached to the ground, cutting them alone is sufficient — no need to physically move the branches. Their cutting is their preparation.
Shmuel
Cutting alone is not enough. One must also move the branches — performing a visible act showing detachment from the ground — to render the sukka fit.
Rav Huna
Both Rav and Shmuel stated this halakha — Shmuel agrees with Rav that cutting suffices as preparation.
Rav Yosef
Did I say Shmuel did not say it? Of course Rav said it — and Shmuel said it as well. That was my point all along!
Core Question Does cutting itself constitute the required act of preparation (תיקון), or must one perform an additional physical action to demonstrate detachment?

📜 Mishna: The Rule of Valid Schach

כל שאינו מקבל טומאה וגידולו מן הארץ — כשר לסכך בו
Mishna Principle Valid schach requires two conditions:

1. Not susceptible to ritual impurity (no vessels, no animal hides)
2. Its growth is from the ground (branches, reeds, wood)

Climbing plants (grapevine, gourd, kissos/ivy) trellised over the sukka while still attached to the ground → sukka is unfit.

If valid schach exceeds invalid portions — the sukka is fit.

🛏️ Tent-Within-a-Tent: The Bridal Canopy

Is there a bed canopy or netting (כילה) inside the sukka?
Inclined (slanted) roof — no flat top
Not considered a tent
Sukka is valid
Permitted per Shmuel and Rav Yehuda
Flat roof, ten+ handbreadths high
Constitutes a tent within a tent
Obligation unfulfilled
Majority rabbinic view
↓
Rabba bar Rav Huna: Even a flat-roofed canopy over ten handbreadths is permitted — follows Rabbi Yehuda, who holds a temporary interior dwelling does not invalidate the sukka.
↓
Fixed bed posts (naklitin): Even with an inclined sheet, the fixed posts render the covering a tent — because they are permanent structures like a house.

🪵 Naklitin vs. Kinofot — Two Kinds of Bed Posts

Naklitin — 2 Posts

  • Two posts fixed in the bed frame
  • Sheet spread over them creates an inclined roof
  • Posts are fixed — sheet is considered a tent structure
  • Yet the sukka itself remains FIT
  • Sleeping inside is problematic — posts create interior tent

Kinofot — 4 Posts

  • Four posts forming a complete frame
  • Sheet spread over four posts creates a flat roof
  • Full tent structure — tent within a tent
  • Sukka is UNFIT as a result
  • Sleeping inside does not fulfill the obligation

☁️ Source for Schach Laws — Two Derivations

Opinion: Clouds of Glory
The sukkot in 'I made the children of Israel dwell in sukkot' (Lev. 23:43) refer to Clouds of Glory. Schach must mirror that source — natural, unprocessed material.
Opinion: Actual Booths
The verse refers to actual physical booths built by Israel in the desert. The schach rules must be derived elsewhere — not from this verse.
Rav Dimi (citing R. Yoḥanan)
'You shall prepare for yourself the Festival of Sukkot' (Deut. 16:13) — sukka is likened to the Festival peace-offering: just as the offering must be valid, schach must come from valid material (not impurity-susceptible, grown from ground).
Key Verse חַג הַסֻּכֹּת תַּעֲשֶׂה לְךָ — 'You shall make for yourself the Festival of Sukkot' (Devarim 16:13)
