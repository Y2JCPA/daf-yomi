Onkelos Consults the Dead: Who Matters Most?

Onkelos
To Balaam: Who is most important in the next world?
Balaam
The Jewish people. But do NOT seek their peace or welfare! (Deut. 23:7)
Onkelos
To Jesus the Nazarene: Who is most important?
Jesus
The Jewish people. Seek their welfare! Anyone who touches them touches the apple of God's eye (Zech. 2:12)
Gemara
See the difference: Balaam (prophet of the nations) wished Israel harm; Jesus (a Jewish sinner) sought their well-being.
Key Insight Even those punished in the next world affirm Israel's primacy — but a prophet of the nations counsels harm, while a Jewish sinner counsels kindness.

The Destruction of Tur Malka (King's Mountain)

🐔 Custom: Rooster & hen led before bride and groom as a fertility blessing
↓
⚔️ Roman soldiers seize the rooster and hen
↓
👊 Residents beat the soldiers
↓
Soldiers tell Emperor: 'The Jews have rebelled!'
↓
Bar Deroma fights
Jumps a mil, kills many Romans — emperor prays for help
Bar Deroma falls
Quotes Psalms 60:12 as fact (not question) — dies via snake in outhouse
David vs. Bar Deroma David said 'Have You not rejected us, O God?' as a question. Bar Deroma said it as a statement of fact — and it became his undoing.

The Emperor's Dilemma at Tur Malka

Round 1: Mercy

  • Bar Deroma dies by snake — not the emperor's hand
  • Emperor says: 'A miracle was performed for me'
  • He lets the people be and departs

Round 2: Wrath

  • Jews celebrate — light candles visible a mil away
  • Emperor: 'The Jews are rejoicing over me!'
  • Returns: 300,000 soldiers massacre for 3 days and nights
  • On the other side of the mountain — weddings continued, unaware
R' Asi The mountain was so vast that one side was being massacred while the other side celebrated — and they did not know about each other.

Nebuzaradan & the Blood of Zechariah

🩸 Nebuzaradan finds blood bubbling up from the ground in the Temple
↓
🐑 They claim it is sacrificial blood — he tests animal blood and it doesn't match
↓
😰 They confess: It is the blood of the prophet Zechariah, whom they killed
↓
⚔️ He kills members of the Great & Lesser Sanhedrin — blood doesn't settle
↓
⚔️ He kills young men, virgins, and schoolchildren — still unsettled
↓
🗣️ He cries: 'Zechariah! I've killed the best of them — shall I destroy them ALL?'
↓
✅ The blood finally settles
↓
🔄 Nebuzaradan reasons: If ONE soul caused this — what awaits ME? He converts to Judaism
Converts from Enemies Naaman was a ger toshav (resident alien). Nebuzaradan was a full convert. Haman's descendants studied Torah in Bnei Brak. Shemaya & Avtalyon descended from Sennacherib.
