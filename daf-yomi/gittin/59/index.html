Gittin 59: Mipnei Darkhei Shalom — For the Ways of Peace

דרכיה דרכי נועם וכל נתיבותיה שלום
KEY THEME This daf explores how social order prevents conflict: from voting procedures in the Sanhedrin, to the unique leadership of Rabbi Yehuda HaNasi, to a sweeping series of enactments mipnei darkhei shalom — governing irrigation rights, trapped animals, lost items, gleaning, and the order of Torah reading. The underlying question: when does 'ways of peace' indicate Torah law vs. rabbinic enactment?

Why Did Rav Vote First in Rebbi's Court?

Rav
I was present for the vote in Rabbi Yehuda HaNasi's court — they started with me first, though I was the youngest
Gemara
But the Mishna (Sanhedrin 32a) says only in capital cases do juniors speak first! This wasn't a capital case!
Rabba b. Rava
Rabbi Yehuda HaNasi's court was different — they always began with junior members
Gemara
Why? Because Rebbi was held in such high esteem that once he spoke, no one would dare contradict him
PRINCIPLE The voting order was adjusted to protect intellectual independence — even senior scholars might be swayed by Rebbi's authority

Deaf-Mute Transactions: Romez vs. Kofetz

Tanna Kamma

  • Deaf-mute may transact through gestures (romez)
  • Can signal to buy or sell — transaction is valid
  • Can respond to others' proposals through gestures
  • Applies to movable property

Ben Beteira

  • Even lip movements (kofetz) suffice
  • No need for hand signals
  • More lenient standard of communication
  • Also valid for movable property transactions
ALSO ENACTED Purchases made by young children (pa'otot) are likewise valid — both enactments made for the betterment of the world

Sources for Kohen Reading First

📖 Rav Mattana: 'Moses delivered it to the priests, the sons of Levi' (Deut 31:9) — priests listed before Levites
↓
📖 R' Yitzchak Nappacha: 'The priests, the sons of Levi, shall come near' (Deut 21:5) — priests come first
↓
📖 Rav Ashi: 'Aaron was separated to be sanctified as most holy' (I Chron 23:13) — holier than other Levites
↓
📖 R' Chiyya bar Abba: 'V'kidashto — And you shall sanctify him' (Lev 21:8) — priority in all sanctity
↓
🏫 School of R' Yishmael: V'kidashto means: open discussion first, lead bentching, choose portion at meals first
KEY VERSE וקדשתו — 'And you shall sanctify him' — for every matter of sanctity: first to speak, first to bless, first to choose
