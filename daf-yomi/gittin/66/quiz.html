Gittin 66 Quiz

5 questions · Self-graded · Test your learning!
QUESTION 1 OF 5
According to Rav Huna, what happens to a shechiv mera's get if he recovers?
A. It remains valid
B. It is revoked, just like his gift
C. It requires new witnesses
D. It must be rewritten
Correct: B — Rav Huna draws a parallel between a deathbed get and a deathbed gift — just as the gift is revoked upon recovery, so too the get is revoked.
QUESTION 2 OF 5
How does the Gemara determine that a voice from a pit is human and not a demon?
A. By seeing the person's face clearly
B. By asking the voice to recite Shema
C. By verifying the being has a shadow of a shadow (בבואה דבבואה)
D. By checking if the voice knows the wife's name
Correct: C — R' Ḥanina taught in the name of his son Yonatan: demons have a shadow but do not have a shadow of a shadow.
QUESTION 3 OF 5
According to Rabbi Meir, what is the status of three people told 'Give a get to my wife'?
A. They are witnesses only
B. They are agents who must write it themselves
C. They constitute a beit din and may delegate the writing
D. They must each write a separate get
Correct: C — R' Meir holds that designating three people with 'Give' creates a court, and a court has authority to instruct others to write the document.
QUESTION 4 OF 5
What is Rabbi Yosei's position on verbal directives (milei)?
A. They can be freely delegated to any agent
B. They can only be delegated to a beit din
C. They cannot be delegated to another agent
D. They require written confirmation
Correct: C — R' Yosei holds מילי לא מימסרן לשליח — verbal directives cannot be transferred to another agent. Even the Sanhedrin would need to write the get themselves.
QUESTION 5 OF 5
If a husband tells ten people 'All of you write and give a get,' and one of them dies before signing, what happens?
A. The remaining nine can complete it
B. The get is null and void
C. Two of the remaining sign as witnesses
D. A replacement must be found
Correct: B — Because the husband specified 'all of you,' each person's participation is essential. If any one of them cannot participate, the get is invalid.
