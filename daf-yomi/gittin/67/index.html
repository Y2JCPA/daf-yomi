Gittin 67 — Agency, Competence & the Limits of Delegation

CORE THEME This daf explores when verbal instructions can be passed through agents, the praise of great Sages and their unique qualities, rules for instructing multiple agents to write a get, and critically — when a person lacks legal competence due to insanity (kordeyakos) or muteness.
3 / 9

Can Verbal Directives Be Delegated to an Agent?

R' Yosei
Verbal directives cannot be delegated to an agent (milei lo mimseran l'shaliach). The husband must personally appoint the scribe and witnesses.
R' Meir
Verbal directives can be delegated — an agent can relay the husband's instructions to a scribe.
R' Chanina of Ono
I agree with R' Meir — delegation of verbal instructions is valid.
Shmuel (citing Rebbi)
The halakha follows R' Yosei. His reasoning (nimmuko) always accompanies his statements.
R' Shimon b. Rebbi
Father — R' Meir and R' Chanina disagree! What led you to rule like R' Yosei?
Rebbi
Be silent, my son! You didn't see R' Yosei. If you had, you'd know — his reasoning always accompanies him.
HALAKHA Shmuel agrees with R' Yosei that verbal directives cannot be delegated, but disagrees on explicit transfer instructions — if the husband said 'tell another to write,' Shmuel permits the transfer.

'Tell the Scribe to Write & You Sign It' — Valid?

Valid but Should Not Be Done

  • Rav Ḥisda — valid but not l'khatchila
  • Rav Naḥman — valid but not l'khatchila
  • Rabba — valid but not l'khatchila
  • Concern: woman may hire false witnesses

Valid and May Be Done

  • Rabba bar bar Ḥana — valid and permitted ab initio
  • Rav Sheshet — valid and permitted ab initio
  • No additional concern beyond normal agency
CONCERN Rav Naḥman explains: We fear the woman might hire witnesses to instruct a scribe without the husband's knowledge. Though witnesses wouldn't forge directly, they might unknowingly relay fraudulent instructions.

Instructing Ten People to Write a Get

Husband says to ten people:
'Write a get'
One writes on behalf of all
'ALL of you write'
One writes in the presence of all
'Deliver a get'
One delivers on behalf of all
'ALL of you deliver'
One delivers in the presence of all
↓
Dilemma: What if he enumerated them by name?
Rav Huna
Enumerated by name ≠ 'all of you'
R' Yochanan
Enumerated by name = 'all of you'
RAVA'S FIX Rava improved the formula: Say 'each of you may write, every two may sign, each may deliver' — avoid saying 'all of you' to prevent invalidation if one person is absent.

Testing the Mute Husband's Competence

🤐 Husband becomes mute after being competent
↓
👥 Two people ask: 'Shall we write a get for your wife?'
↓
🔄 He nods his head in agreement
↓
🔍 They examine him three times with test questions
↓
✅ He answers 'No' to negatives and 'Yes' to positives correctly
↓
📜 They write and deliver the get based on his nod
KEY RULE The three-part examination must show consistent, accurate responses — confirming the husband understands the question and is competent despite being unable to speak.
