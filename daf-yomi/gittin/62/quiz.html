Gittin 62 Quiz

5 questions · Self-graded · Test your learning!
QUESTION 1 OF 5
Why does Rav Yosef say the wife of an am ha'aretz takes food while preparing it?
A. She rationalizes: 'The ox may eat from its threshing'
B. She has explicit permission from her husband
C. The Sages permitted it for shalom bayit
D. She is not aware of the prohibition
Correct: A — Rav Yosef explains she self-justifies by comparing herself to an ox that eats while threshing — she feels entitled to eat while working, which is different from deliberately exchanging food.
QUESTION 2 OF 5
Why was the leniency for a chaver preparing challah/teruma for an am ha'aretz granted?
A. To prevent the am ha'aretz from sinning
B. For the sake of the kneader's and olive presser's livelihood
C. Because the am ha'aretz pays extra for purity
D. To encourage am ha'aretz to become chaverim
Correct: B — R' Yochanan states the leniency was granted for the sake of the livelihood (parnasa) of the kneader and olive presser, who would otherwise lose business from am ha'aretz clients.
QUESTION 3 OF 5
Why does the Gemara trust that the am ha'aretz won't touch the challah despite the warning?
A. Because am ha'aretz are generally honest about challah
B. Because there is a financial penalty for touching it
C. Because his hiring a chaver shows he wants things done properly
D. Because a beit din supervises the transfer
Correct: C — The Gemara reasons that since the am ha'aretz specifically sought out a chaver to prepare his dough in purity, he clearly cares about proper observance and will heed the warning.
QUESTION 4 OF 5
What is the key legal difference between an agent of receipt (שליח קבלה) and an agent of delivery (שליח הולכה)?
A. Agent of receipt must be a Torah scholar; agent of delivery need not be
B. Agent of receipt effectuates divorce upon receiving the get; agent of delivery only upon reaching the wife
C. Agent of delivery works for the wife; agent of receipt works for the husband
D. Agent of receipt can only be appointed by the husband
Correct: B — An agent of receipt (appointed by the wife) makes the divorce effective the moment he receives the get. An agent of delivery (the husband's agent) only completes the divorce when the get reaches the wife herself.
QUESTION 5 OF 5
According to Rabban Shimon ben Gamliel, what happens when a wife says 'Take (טלי) my get for me'?
A. The agent becomes an agent of delivery only
B. The designation is invalid and must be restated
C. The agent is considered an agent of receipt — husband cannot retract
D. The husband must confirm the appointment before it takes effect
Correct: C — Rabban Shimon ben Gamliel holds that even the word 'take' (טלי) creates an agent of receipt, meaning the divorce takes effect immediately upon the agent receiving the get, and the husband cannot retract.
