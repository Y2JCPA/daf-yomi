When Must a Bleeding Woman Examine Herself?

Woman bleeds during intercourse with 1st husband — 3 times
↓
Must divorce and marry another man
↓
Bleeds with 2nd husband — 3 times
↓
Must divorce again and marry another
↓
Bleeds with 3rd husband — 3 times
↓
Now has presumptive status (חזקה) — must examine herself
RATIONALE R' Yochanan: She should not examine early because "not all fingers are equal" — a different husband may not cause bleeding.

The Tube Examination (שפופרת)

1️⃣ Take a tube of lead (Shmuel) with the end folded inward to prevent scratching
↓
2️⃣ Place a cosmetic brush (מכחול) inside, long enough to reach deeply
↓
3️⃣ Wrap an absorbent cloth (מוך) on the tip of the brush
↓
4️⃣ Insert the tube and then remove it
↓
✅ Blood on top of cloth → from uterus → טמאה (impure)
↓
✅ No blood on top → from vaginal walls → טהורה (pure)
NOTE Shmuel specifies a lead tube with the mouth folded inward — other materials would scratch and cause misleading bleeding.

Blood from a Wound in the Uterus

R' Yehuda HaNasi
A woman is deemed credible to say: I have a wound in my uterus from where the blood emerges. She is pure.
R' Shimon b. Gamliel
Blood from a wound that passes through the uterus is impure as a primary category of impurity — even though she may remain with her husband.
The Sages
We have a tradition: blood of a wound from the uterus is ritually pure.
Ulla
The dispute is whether the place of the uterus renders all blood passing through it impure, or only blood that originates there.
KEY DISTINCTION R' Shimon b. Gamliel: the uterus is an impure place — any blood passing through becomes impure. The Sages: only blood originating in the uterus is impure.

Reish Lakish Challenges R' Yochanan

Reish Lakish
Why not examine after the 3rd time with the 1st husband? Why must she divorce?
R' Yochanan
"Not all fingers are equal" — a different husband may not cause bleeding. Better to remarry than risk being forbidden to all men.
Reish Lakish
Then why not examine after the 1st time with the 3rd husband? She already has a חזקה!
R' Yochanan
"Not all forces are equal" — the manner of intercourse varies. Only after 3 times is the presumption established.
ANECDOTE Rabbi Yehuda HaNasi once told Abdon to frighten a woman with this condition — a mass of congealed blood fell out and she was cured!
