Niddah 48: Signs of Maturity & How We Count Years

The Gemara asks: How do we calculate 'years' for various halakhot?
↓
A baraita teaches that years of a son and daughter are counted as full years (date to date)
↓
Rav
Refers to valuations (Arakhin) — terms written in Torah
Rav Yosef
Refers to Yotze Dofen chapter — ages in our Mishnayot
↓
They do not disagree — each stated a different application
↓
MISHNA: The lower sign (two pubic hairs) alone establishes adulthood for women
Key Principle The Torah equates women to men for signs of majority — just as a man needs only the lower sign, so does a woman.

What Does 'Years of a Son and Daughter' Mean?

Rav Giddel / Rav
It refers to valuations (Arakhin) — the age of a valuated person is calculated in full years from birth date
Rav Yosef
It refers to the halakhot in our chapter Yotze Dofen — when ages are mentioned, they mean full years
Abaye
Do you and Rav actually disagree on this?
Rav Yosef
No — he said one thing, I said another. We do not disagree.
Gemara
This makes sense — Rav himself says all ages in this chapter are full years, so both applications are valid
Resolution Rav chose valuations because the baraita lists Torah-written categories; Rav Yosef chose Yotze Dofen because the terms 'son' and 'daughter' match that chapter's language.

Lower Sign vs. Upper Sign: Which Determines Adulthood?

A young woman shows signs of physical development
↓
Lower sign first
Two pubic hairs appear before breast development → She is an adult
Upper sign first
Breasts develop before pubic hairs appear → Dispute between R' Meir and Rabbis
↓
Source: "A man or woman, when they commit any of the sins of men" (Numbers 5:6)
↓
Torah equates woman to man — just as a man needs only the lower sign, so does a woman
R' Eliezer b. R' Tzadok The Sages of Yavne taught: Once the lower sign appears, we no longer concern ourselves with the upper sign.

City Girls vs. Village Girls: Environmental Factors

City Dwellers

  • Lower sign (pubic hair) appears first
  • Reason: They frequent the bathhouses
  • Warm water stimulates hair growth

Village Dwellers

  • Upper sign (breasts) appears first
  • Reason: They grind with mills
  • Physical labor develops the chest
Rabban Shimon ben Gamliel The Sages recognized that environment and lifestyle affect the pace and order of physical maturation.

Women Examining Girls for Signs of Puberty

👩‍⚕️ All girls examined for puberty signs are examined by women
↓
🏛️ R' Eliezer gave girls to his wife to examine; R' Yishmael gave them to his mother
↓
📅 Before age 11+1 day: Any hair is a mole, not a sign — women may examine
↓
📅 After age 12+1 day: She is presumed adult — women may examine
↓
⚠️ Between 11+1 and 12+1: R' Yehuda says women may NOT examine (uncertain period)
↓
✅ R' Shimon says women MAY examine even then — credible to be stringent but not lenient
Stringent but Not Lenient If a woman says the girl reached majority → she cannot perform refusal (mi'un). If the woman says she's a minor → she cannot perform ḥalitza. But not vice versa.
