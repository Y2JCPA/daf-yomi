The Journey of a Fetus: From Formation to Birth

🌱 Months 1–3: Fetus resides in lower compartment of womb — being 'woven together' (Psalms 139:15)
↓
📈 Months 4–6: Fetus moves to middle compartment
↓
⬆️ Months 7–9: Fetus rises to upper compartment — the heavier it grows, the higher it ascends
↓
🔄 Time of Birth: Fetus turns upside down and emerges — this causes labor pains
↓
📖 Day of Birth: Fetus is administered an oath (gozi — Psalms 71:6)
KEY INSIGHT R' Elazar compares the fetus to a nut floating in a basin of water — easily tipped in any direction by the lightest touch.

Fetal Development: Three Trimesters

⬇️ First trimester: Fetus in lower compartment — 'woven together in the lowest parts of the earth' (Psalms 139:15)
↓
➡️ Second trimester: Fetus moves to middle compartment
↓
⬆️ Third trimester: Fetus in upper compartment
↓
🔄 Birth: Fetus turns upside down and emerges — causing labor pains
↓
👧 Female fetus must also turn its face around (faces upward), causing greater labor pains
R' ELAZAR The verse 'I was woven together in the lowest parts of the earth' (Psalms 139:15) teaches the fetus begins in the lower compartment.

Three Partners in the Creation of a Person

PartnerContribution
Father (white seed)Bones, sinews, nails, brain, white of the eye
Mother (red seed)Skin, flesh, hair, black of the eye
HaKadosh Baruch HuSpirit, soul, countenance (kelaster), eyesight, hearing, speech, walking, understanding, wisdom
RAV PAPPA When a person dies, God retrieves His part. As the saying goes: 'Remove the salt from meat, and you may toss it to a dog' — without God's contribution, the body is worthless.

God's Ways vs. Human Ways (R' Ḥinnana bar Pappa)

Flesh & Blood

  • Puts an item in a sealed flask, opening upward — still uncertain if it's preserved
  • Places items on a scale — the heavier it is, the more it descends

HaKadosh Baruch Hu

  • Forms a fetus in an open womb, opening facing downward — yet it is preserved!
  • The heavier the fetus grows, the more it ascends upward in the womb!
SOURCE 'Who does great deeds beyond comprehension, wondrous deeds without number' (Job 9:10)

When Does a Woman Conceive?

R' Yitzḥak / R' Ami
A woman becomes pregnant only by intercourse close to the onset of her menstrual cycle — as it says: 'Behold, I was brought forth in iniquity' (Psalms 51:7)
R' Yoḥanan
A woman becomes pregnant only near the time of her immersion in a mikveh — 'And in sin [uvḥet] did my mother conceive me' (Psalms 51:7)
Gemara
How do we know ḥet means purity? As it says regarding leprosy of houses: 'Veḥittei the house' (Lev. 14:52) — translated as 'he shall purify the house'
NOTE Both opinions derive from the same verse (Psalms 51:7) but interpret different words — 'iniquity' vs. 'sin/ḥet.'

R' Shimon ben Yoḥai: Why a Post-Birth Offering?

🤰 Woman in intense labor pain
↓
📢 She impulsively swears an oath: 'I will never be intimate with my husband again!'
↓
💑 She later resumes relations with her husband, violating her oath
↓
🕊️ Torah requires her to bring a korban (offering) after childbirth
RAV YOSEF OBJECTS She violates intentionally! And she should bring a female lamb or goat (the standard oath-violation offering), not a bird offering!
