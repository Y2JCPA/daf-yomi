Rosh Hashanah 25 Quiz

5 questions · Self-graded · Test your learning!
QUESTION 1 OF 5
Why did Rabban Gamliel command Rabbi Yehoshua to appear before him with his staff and money?
A. To show Rabbi Yehoshua that carrying objects is permitted on Rosh Hashanah
B. To publicly demonstrate that Rabbi Yehoshua accepted the court's calendar ruling, even if it meant 'desecrating' his own Yom Kippur
C. To punish Rabbi Yehoshua for testifying falsely
D. To invite Rabbi Yehoshua to serve as a judge in his court
Correct: B — Carrying a staff and money would violate Yom Kippur if Rabbi Yehoshua's date were correct. By complying, he publicly acknowledged Rabban Gamliel's declaration as binding.
QUESTION 2 OF 5
What is the halachic principle derived from the word אתם ('you') in Leviticus 23:4?
A. Only the witnesses themselves can determine the new month
B. The calendar must always follow astronomical observation
C. The Beit Din's declaration is binding even if made in error, intentionally, or under deception
D. Only a unanimous court can sanctify the new month
Correct: C — Rabbi Akiva cites 'which you shall proclaim' — אתם implies the court's authority is absolute. The Talmud lists: even if שוגגין (erring), מזידין (intentional), or מוטעין (misled).
QUESTION 3 OF 5
What was Rabbi Dosa ben Horkinas's argument in support of Rabban Gamliel?
A. He testified that he personally saw the new moon in agreement with the witnesses
B. He proved astronomically that the witnesses were correct
C. Questioning Rabban Gamliel's court would invalidate every court back to Moses and Aaron
D. He cited a tradition from Sinai permitting flexible calendar decisions
Correct: C — Rabbi Dosa argued that undermining one valid Beit Din undermines the entire chain of judicial authority throughout Jewish history — a devastating precedent.
QUESTION 4 OF 5
What did Rabban Gamliel say when Rabbi Yehoshua arrived before him after complying with the decree?
A. 'You are forgiven for your rebellious behavior'
B. 'Come in peace, my teacher in wisdom and my student in accepting my words'
C. 'The halacha follows your position after all'
D. 'Let us recalculate the new month together'
Correct: B — Rabban Gamliel rose and kissed Rabbi Yehoshua, calling him both teacher (acknowledging his wisdom) and student (for submitting to authority) — a beautiful resolution honoring both.
QUESTION 5 OF 5
Who initially declared the witnesses to be false witnesses based on astronomical impossibility?
A. Rabbi Akiva
B. Rabbi Dosa ben Horkinas
C. Rabbi Yehoshua
D. Rabbi Yochanan ben Nuri
Correct: D — Rabbi Yochanan ben Nuri declared the witnesses false because the moon could not have appeared in the location they described — their testimony was astronomically impossible.
