⚖️ The Great Calendar Dispute

Rabban Gamliel
I accepted the witnesses' testimony. The court's ruling is final. Come before me with your staff and money on the day you calculate to be Yom Kippur.
Rabbi Yehoshua
The witnesses are false — it is impossible for the moon to appear so soon after the waning moon. I cannot desecrate what I believe to be Yom Kippur.
Rabbi Akiva
The Torah says 'which you shall proclaim' — you, even if you err; you, even intentionally; you, even if misled by false witnesses. The court's word is binding.
Rabbi Dosa ben Horkinas
If we question Rabban Gamliel's court, we must question every court since Moses. The office of head of the Sanhedrin carries absolute authority — we must submit.
Resolution Rabbi Yehoshua took his staff and money and walked to Yavne on the day he calculated to be Yom Kippur. Rabban Gamliel rose, kissed him, and said: 'Come in peace, my teacher and my student.' The court's authority stands supreme.

📜 The Power of Atem — You Shall Proclaim

אַתֶּם — אֲפִלּוּ שׁוֹגְגִין, אַתֶּם — אֲפִלּוּ מְזִידִין, אַתֶּם — אֲפִלּוּ מוּטְעִין
Vayikra 23:2 — מוֹעֲדֵי ה׳ אֲשֶׁר תִּקְרְאוּ אֹתָם "The appointed seasons of the Lord, which you shall proclaim" — The word אֹתָם (otam, them) is read as אַתֶּם (atem, you), teaching that the court alone holds calendar authority:

• אַתֶּם — Even if you erred unwittingly (שׁוֹגְגִין)
• אַתֶּם — Even if you acted intentionally (מְזִידִין)
• אַתֶּם — Even if you were misled by false witnesses (מוּטְעִין)

In all cases, the court's proclamation is valid. No outside authority — not even knowledge that witnesses lied — can undo what the court has sanctified.

🏛️ Jephthah in His Generation = Samuel in His Generation

The Verse

  • "You shall come to the judge who shall be in those days" (Deut. 17:9)
  • Can you go to a judge not alive in your days? Obviously not!
  • Therefore: you must go to whoever is the judge of your generation
  • That judge carries the full weight and authority of the office

The Greats

  • ⚖️ Moses — Greatest prophet and lawgiver
  • 🏺 Aaron — High Priest, leader alongside Moses
  • 🎶 Samuel — Prophet and last of the Judges; Psalms 99:6 equates him to Moses and Aaron

The Equals (in their day)

  • ⚔️ Jerubaal (Gideon) in his generation = Moses
  • 🛡️ Bedan in his generation = Aaron
  • 🗡️ Jephthah in his generation = Samuel
  • The verse equates them — the role grants authority, not personal greatness

🌑 Can the Court Sanctify the New Month?

Court (and/or all Israel) observes the new moon on the 29th night
↓
Did the court complete the declaration 'Sanctified!' before nightfall?
Yes — declared before nightfall
Month is 29 days (incomplete month). The new month begins.
No — nightfall passed first
Previous month is automatically 30 days (full month). Cannot sanctify retroactively.
↓
Why can't witnesses skip interrogation just because all saw it? — Court must act judicially, not on personal knowledge; also prevents leniency via self-testimony
↓
Rosh HaShana is called both chag (festival) and mishpat (judgment) — unlike monetary cases, calendar law cannot be extended as if still in session after nightfall
