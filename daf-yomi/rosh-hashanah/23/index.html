🔦 Why the Torch System Could Not Be Fixed

Torch relay system: announce New Moon across the land
↓
Problem: New Moon falls on Shabbat eve — torches cannot be lit
↓
Fix #1: Light for both full & deficient; skip Shabbat conflicts
Rejected: people say 'the month is full, they were just prevented by circumstances'
Fix #2: Light only for full months, never for deficient
Rejected by Abaye: people will confuse a deficient month for a full one
↓
All proposed workarounds lead to public error — torches replaced by human messengers
3 / 9

🔦 Can the Torch System Be Salvaged?

Gemara
Let them light torches for both full and deficient months. When the New Moon falls on Shabbat eve, simply skip the ceremony entirely.
Rebuttal
People will say: 'The month is full — they just could not light the torches due to circumstances beyond their control.' Confusion remains.
Gemara
Then light torches only for a full, thirty-day month and never for a deficient one. No ceremony = deficient month, always unambiguous!
Abaye
This too leads to error: people witnessing no torches during a full month delayed by Shabbat will mistakenly treat it as a deficient month.
Result No modification of the torch system could eliminate public confusion. The Sages replaced torches with human messengers entirely.

🌲 How Many Species of Cedar?

Rav Yehuda

  • 4 species of cedar
  • Erez (cedar)
  • Katrom
  • Armón (plane tree)
  • Brosh (cypress)
  • Used for the kelonsot (long torch poles)

Rabba bar Rav Huna
School of Rav

  • 10 species of cedar
  • Based on Isaiah 41:19
  • Seven named in that verse
  • Rav Dimi (from Eretz Israel) added:
  • Terebinth, Oak, Coral wood
  • Coral wood = tzi adir

🪸 Harvesting Coral — The Tzi Adir Expedition

Source: Isaiah 33:21 — "Neither shall a tzi adir be able to cross it" — Rav identifies this as coral, a prized timber
↓
Mobilize 6,000 men working for 12 months — or alternatively, 12,000 men for 6 months
↓
Load a ship with sand as ballast until the hull sinks deep into the sea
↓
Lower iron chains down to the sea floor and dredge up the coral growth
↓
Coral is exchanged for twice its weight in silver — among the most valuable commodities
↓
Three ports exist in those regions: two Roman, one Persian; coral trade passes through all three

☀️ The Sun and Moon — Cosmic Peace

מַשְׁלִ֣ית וָפַ֣חַד עִמּ֑וֹ עֹשֶׂ֥ה שָׁ֝ל֗וֹם בִּמְרוֹמָֽיו — איוב כה:ב
Rabbi Yoḥanan on Job 25:2 "Dominion and fear are with Him; He makes peace in His high places."

The sun has never seen the concave (blemished) side of the new moon — nor has it ever seen the concave side of the rainbow.

God arranges the heavens so that neither celestial body witnesses the other's moment of diminishment, preserving dignity and shalom on high.

This is also connected to the court's examination of moon witnesses: the witnesses describe the moon's position relative to the sun, raising the question of whether the sun and moon face each other at all.
