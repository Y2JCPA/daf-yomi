📍 Tiberias — the Lowest

מִמַּעֲמַקִּים קְרָאתִיךָ ה׳ — תהלים קל:א
Verse Fulfilled Tiberias sits in the lowest geographical location in the Land of Israel. The verse: "From the depths I called to You, O God" (Psalms 130:1) — מִמַּעֲמַקִּים קְרָאתִיךָ ה׳ — is fulfilled by the Sanhedrin's final exile to this lowest place.

🐏 Shofar on Shabbat — Where May It Be Blown?

Mishna
When Rosh Hashanah falls on Shabbat, the shofar is blown only in the place where the Great Sanhedrin (בית דין הגדול) sits — not everywhere.
R' Yochanan b. Zakkai
After the destruction, I enact: wherever there is a rabbinic court (בית דין), the shofar may be blown on Shabbat-Rosh Hashanah.
R' Eliezer
R' Yochanan ben Zakkai instituted this for Yavneh specifically — it applies to Yavneh alone, not to every beit din!
Sages
No — he instituted it for any place that has a beit din, just as Yavneh did. The principle is the presence of a qualified court.
Ruling The halacha follows the Sages: any city with a proper beit din may blow the shofar on Shabbat-Rosh Hashanah.
