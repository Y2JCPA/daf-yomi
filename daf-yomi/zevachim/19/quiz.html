Zevachim 19 Quiz

5 questions · Self-graded · Test your learning!
QUESTION 1 OF 5
According to R' Yochanan, when is an extra item on a priest's body considered a forbidden 'extra garment'?
A. Only where the vestments are normally worn
B. Anywhere on the body
C. Only on the hands
D. Only if it is made of linen
Correct: A — R' Yochanan holds that extra garments are only problematic in a place on the body where the requisite vestments are worn. In other locations, the item is not considered an extra garment.
QUESTION 2 OF 5
According to Rava/Rav Chisda, what is the interposition rule for a place where vestments are NOT worn?
A. Any item interposes regardless of size
B. Only items ≥ 3×3 fingerbreadths interpose
C. Nothing interposes regardless of size
D. Only metal items interpose
Correct: B — Rava says that Rav Chisda says: Where vestments are not worn, fabric of 3×3 fingerbreadths or more interposes, but less than that does not — with the exception of a significant item like a sash.
QUESTION 3 OF 5
If a High Priest on Yom Kippur failed to sanctify his hands and feet between changing from golden to white garments, what is the status of his service?
A. The service is disqualified
B. The service is valid after the fact
C. He must repeat the entire Yom Kippur service
D. Only the next rite is disqualified
Correct: B — The baraita teaches that the inter-garment sanctifications on Yom Kippur are valid after the fact if omitted. Only the initial morning sanctification is indispensable and would disqualify the service.
QUESTION 4 OF 5
Why does Chizkiyya NOT derive the Basin's water requirement from the verse 'That Moshe and Aharon and his sons might wash' (Shemot 40:31)?
A. He derives the law for future generations from his own verse instead
B. He needs that verse for R' Yosei b'R Chanina's teaching that the Basin must hold water for 4 priests
C. He does not consider that verse authoritative
D. That verse refers only to the Mishkan, not the Temple
Correct: B — Chizkiyya requires R' Yonatan's verse (Shemot 40:31) for the teaching of R' Yosei son of R' Chanina — that the Basin must contain enough water for four priests (Moshe, Aharon, and his two sons).
QUESTION 5 OF 5
What was the Sages' objection to R' Yosei son of R' Yehuda's method of sanctifying hands and feet?
A. It used too much water from the Basin
B. It required stacking all four limbs, which is physically impossible to maintain
C. It was not dignified for Temple service
D. It contradicted an explicit verse
Correct: B — The Sages said 'You have gone too far — it is impossible to do so,' meaning that stacking both hands on both stacked feet would cause the priest to lose balance. Rav Yosef responded that another priest could help him.
