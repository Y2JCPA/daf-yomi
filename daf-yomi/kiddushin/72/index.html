Do Gittin and Lineage Share the Same Borders?

Rav Pappa
Just as Rav and Shmuel dispute the northern border for lineage, so too they dispute it for bills of divorce (gittin). The borders are identical.
Rav Yosef
The dispute is only about lineage. For gittin, everyone agrees Babylonia extends to the second lake of the bridge that Shmuel mentioned.
Abaye
Until where does the border extend on the western side of the Euphrates?
Rav Yosef
Why ask? Even those of pure lineage in Pumbedita marry women from Biram — its residents are presumed fit.
Practical Impact An agent bringing a get from Babylonia need not say 'it was written and signed in my presence' — but the exact boundary of Babylonia was disputed.

Identifying the Exile Locations of the Ten Tribes

Verse (II Kings 18:11)

  • Halah — a place of exile
  • Habor — on the river of Gozan
  • Cities of the Medes — eastern regions

R' Abba bar Kahana's Identifications

  • Halah = Ḥillazon
  • Habor = Hadyav
  • River of Gozan = Ginzak
  • Cities of Medes = Ḥamadan (or Nihavnad)

Consequence

  • Ten Tribes assimilated with gentiles
  • Jews from these places have flawed lineage
  • This contradicts Rav who said Ḥillazon Nihavnad is like the exile (unflawed)
Conflict Rav held Ḥillazon Nihavnad had unflawed lineage; R' Abba bar Kahana identified it as a place where the Ten Tribes were exiled — making its lineage flawed.

Ḥillazon Nihavnad: Conflict of Interest?

Rav Ika bar Avin
Rav Ḥananel says in Rav's name: Ḥillazon Nihavnad is like the exile with regard to lineage — its residents are fit.
Abaye
Don't listen to him! A yevama from there fell before him for levirate marriage — he wants to marry her, so he declared the lineage pure!
Rav Ika bar Avin
Is this my halakha? It is Rav Ḥananel's teaching! I cannot be accused of bias when I am merely transmitting.
Rav Ḥananel
(When asked directly) Indeed, Rav said: Ḥillazon Nihavnad is like the exile with regard to lineage.
Lesson The Gemara scrutinizes potential bias — even when a sage transmits a teaching from his master, personal interest raises suspicion. The teaching was verified by going directly to Rav Ḥananel.

Persians Compared to Bears (Daniel 7:5)

🐻 Rav Yosef taught: The 'second beast, similar to a bear' = Persians
↓
🍖 They eat and drink copious amounts like a bear
↓
💪 They are corpulent like a bear
↓
🧔 They grow hair like a bear
↓
🏃 They have no rest like a bear, constantly moving
↓
🦴 The 'three ribs in its mouth' = Ḥillazon, Hadyav, and Netzivin — territories Persia sometimes swallows and sometimes discharges
