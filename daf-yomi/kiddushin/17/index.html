How Much Is the Severance Gift (Ha'anaka)?

R' Meir
5 sela for each type: flock, threshing floor, winepress = 15 sela total. Derived from gezera shava of "empty" — connecting severance to firstborn redemption (5 sela).
R' Yehuda
30 sela total. Derived from gezera shava of "giving" — connecting to the 30-shekel fine paid for a Canaanite slave gored by an ox (Shemot 21:32).
R' Shimon
50 shekel. Like the maximum erech (valuation) for a man (Vayikra 27:3).
R' Meir's Novelty The total is 15 — but the master may give less of one type and more of another, as long as the total is reached.

"Blessed Due to Him" — Is the Gift Conditional?

Tanna Kamma (First Tanna)

  • "הַעֲנֵק תַּעֲנִיק" — doubled verb means: grant in ANY case
  • Even if house was NOT blessed due to slave
  • "Blessed you" only teaches: give proportional to your blessing

R' Elazar ben Azarya

  • Torah spoke in the language of people (doubled verb is stylistic)
  • If house was blessed due to him — grant gift
  • If NOT blessed — no gift at all
  • Takes the verse at face value
Hermeneutic Principle R' Elazar ben Azarya holds the Torah's doubled verbs are ordinary emphasis, not sources for new halakhot.
