Ordinary vs. Tied Documents

שטר פשוט (Ordinary)

  • Witnesses sign inside (written side)
  • Standard format for most documents
  • If witnesses signed on back → invalid

שטר מקושר (Tied/Folded)

  • Witnesses sign on the back
  • Folded and tied with strings
  • If witnesses signed inside → dispute
R' Ḥanina ben Gamliel A tied document with signatures inside is valid — you can simply untie it and it becomes an ordinary document.

Regional Custom vs. Specific Instructions

Gemara
Does the first Tanna really reject following regional custom (מנהג המדינה)?
Rav Ashi
They agree where custom is clear. The dispute is where both types are used and he specified ordinary but got tied.
Tanna Kamma
He was particular (מקפיד) — document is invalid since the scribe deviated.
R' Shimon b. Gamliel
He was merely indicating his preference (מראה מקום) — the document is valid.
R' Elazar
I agree — like a woman who says 'receive my get in Place X' but it's received elsewhere, it's still valid.
Principle מראה מקום הוא לו — When giving instructions, is the person being strict or just suggesting? This question echoes through documents, gittin, and kiddushin.

The Power of a Single Thought

שמא הרהר תשובה בדעתו
Key Halakha "On condition I am righteous" — even if completely wicked, she is betrothed, for perhaps he had thoughts of repentance (הרהורי תשובה).

"On condition I am wicked" — even if completely righteous, she is betrothed, for perhaps he had thoughts of idol worship (הרהורי עבודה זרה).

A single moment of inner intention can transform a person's entire status.
