Kiddushin 75 Quiz

5 questions · Self-graded · Test your learning!
QUESTION 1 OF 5
According to R' Yochanan, what is the practical difference between the First Tanna and R' Yosei?
A. A Jewish woman who married an Ammonite convert
B. A Jewish woman who had relations with a second-generation Egyptian convert
C. A widow who remarried a kohen
D. A woman who married a mamzer
Correct: B — R' Yochanan says the difference involves a second-generation Egyptian convert. The First Tanna says he disqualifies her (transgression), while R' Yosei says he does not (since the offspring would be fit as a third-generation Egyptian).
QUESTION 2 OF 5
Why does Rabban Shimon ben Gamliel hold that a male Ammonite or Moabite convert does NOT disqualify a woman from priesthood?
A. Because Ammonites and Moabites are not real converts
B. Because their female offspring are permitted to enter the congregation
C. Because the Torah never prohibited Ammonites
D. Because their conversion is retroactively valid
Correct: B — RSBG requires that ALL offspring be unfit (male and female) to disqualify. Since 'an Ammonite man is prohibited but not an Ammonite woman,' the female offspring are fit — so he does not disqualify.
QUESTION 3 OF 5
What does Rav Ḥisda teach about a widow of questionable lineage?
A. She is fit to marry a kohen according to all opinions
B. She is unfit according to all the Tannaim in the mishna
C. Only R' Yosei considers her unfit
D. Her status depends on whether her children are fit
Correct: B — Rav Ḥisda says all concede — even the most lenient Tanna (RSBG) — that a widow of questionable lineage is unfit for priesthood. This excludes the more lenient view of R' Yehoshua and R' Yehuda ben Beteira.
QUESTION 4 OF 5
What does the phrase 'lion converts' (geirei arayot) mean regarding the Samaritans?
A. They were as brave as lions in their conversion
B. They converted out of fear of lions, not sincere devotion
C. They worshipped a lion-shaped idol
D. They were descended from the tribe of Judah (whose symbol is a lion)
Correct: B — According to R' Yishmael, the Samaritans converted only because they feared being attacked by lions (as described in II Kings 17) — not out of genuine commitment to Judaism. Therefore they remain gentiles in halakhic status.
QUESTION 5 OF 5
According to R' Akiva's view, why were the Samaritans ultimately disqualified from marrying Jews despite being 'true converts'?
A. They worshipped idols secretly
B. They performed levirate marriage incorrectly — only with betrothed widows
C. Their priests were of flawed lineage
D. They refused to accept the Oral Torah
Correct: B — Even though R' Akiva considers them true converts with fit priests, the Sages disqualified them because they practiced levirate marriage (yibum) incorrectly — performing it only with women widowed from betrothal, not from full marriage.
