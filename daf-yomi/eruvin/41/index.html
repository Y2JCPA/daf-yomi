🕯️ Tisha B'Av on Friday: Must One Complete the Fast?

Rabban Gamliel
One does not need to fast on Shabbat eve when Tisha B'Av falls that day. Shabbat honor takes precedence — eat and drink fully.
Rabbi Yosei
One must fast and complete the fast. Just as when Tisha B'Av falls on Sunday one must stop eating before Shabbat ends, so too here one completes the fast.
Ulla
The halakha follows Rabbi Yosei: on Tisha B'Av that falls on Shabbat eve, one fasts and completes the fast.
Rav Yehuda (citing Shmuel)
The halakha follows the Rabbis. But their ruling was stated only for Hanukkah and Purim — not necessarily for Tisha B'Av on Shabbat eve.
Final Ruling Mar Zutra in the name of Rav Huna: The halakha is that one fasts and completes the fast on the eves of Shabbat and Festivals.

📜 Rabbi Akiva and the Egg — Subtle Mourning on Shabbat

Tisha B'Av fell on Shabbat eve (Friday)
↓
Students sat before Rabbi Akiva in the study hall
↓
They brought him a slightly cooked egg
↓
He swallowed it without salt — a subtle sign of mourning
↓
Not because it was required, but to demonstrate to students that eating on Shabbat is permitted
↓
Lesson: A Torah scholar may personally adopt subtle mourning practices even when full eating is permitted

⛵ The Boat Incident from Pelandarsin

מעשה שהיו באין מפלנדרסין וספינתם יצאת בשבת
Historical Incident — Mishna Four Sages were sailing home from Pelandarsin when their boat sailed on Shabbat, carrying them beyond their limit.

Rabban Gamliel & R. Elazar ben Azarya: Permitted to walk the entire city (enclosed area = full limit).
Rabbi Yehoshua & Rabbi Akiva: Restricted themselves to 4 cubits (stringent opinion).

When the boat could not dock until after nightfall on Friday, Rabban Gamliel said: "I was watching — we entered the city's limit before nightfall. We acquired our resting place during twilight." All were permitted to alight.

😔 Three Rabbinic Lists — Suffering, Atonement, and Warning

Three Who Don't See Gehenna

  • 💸 Those in extreme poverty
  • 🤒 Those afflicted with intestinal disease
  • 👿 Those burdened with an evil wife (some opinions — suffering atones)

Three Who Act Against Their Will

  • 😈 One seized by an evil spirit (temporary insanity)
  • 💸 One crushed by extreme poverty
  • 👿 One trapped with an evil wife

Three Liable to Die Suddenly

  • 🤒 Those with intestinal disease
  • 🏗️ Those engaged in dangerous labor
  • ⚠️ Appear healthy and can converse — but may die at any moment
