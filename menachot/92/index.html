R. Shimon says, just like the blood of the inner goat atones for Yisraelim for Tum'ah (of the Mikdash or Kodshim). The blood of the bull atones for Kohanim for Tum'ah of the Mikdash or Kodshim;
Just like Viduy (confession) over Se'ir ha'Mishtale'ach atones for Yisraelim for other transgressions, Viduy over the bull atones for Kohanim for other transgressions.
Question: Surely, the verse equated the atonement of Kohanim and Yisraelim. How does R. Shimon explain this?
Answer: Indeed, it teaches that they all get atonement for Tum'ah and other transgressions. however, they get atonement through different Korbanos.
Summary: According to R. Yehudah, blood of the inner goat for Yisraelim, and of the bull for Kohanim, atones for Tum'ah. Viduy over Se'ir ha'Mishtale'ach atones for all for other transgressions;
R. Shimon only argues about atonement for Kohanim for other transgressions. He says that also this is through Viduy over the bull.
