Condition Requirement What it Excludes
1. Fit Person The Kohen must be fit for Avodah Ba'al Mum (blemished Kohen)

The practical result: According to Rava, Lo Lishmah intent on the Omer simply has no effect at all. The offering is fully Kosher, the remainder can be eaten, and no second Omer is needed. This is the most lenient of the three views.
