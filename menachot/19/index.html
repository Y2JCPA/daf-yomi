Is Yetzikah (Pouring Oil) Kohen-Only?

Chachamim

  • "v'Hevi'ah El Bnei Aharon v'Kamatz" — only from Kemitzah and onward requires a Kohen
  • Yetzikah and Belilah (before Kemitzah): non-Kohen is valid

R. Shimon

  • The "vav" in "ve'Hevi'ah" adds Yetzikah to "Bnei Aharon"
  • Yav Mosif — the vav connects the prior act to the current requirement
  • Yetzikah requires a Kohen — non-Kohen is invalid!
Abaye clarifies: The underlying debate is whether "Mikra Nidrash l'Fanav ul'Acharav" — a verse is expounded regarding what comes before AND after. Chachamim: only forward; R. Shimon uses the vav to connect backward.

Rav's Rule: Torah and Chukah Signal Me'akev

Rav's major principle: wherever the Torah uses "Torah" or "Chukah" in context of an act, that act is Me'akev

OfferingWord usedMe'akev?
Nazir — Tenufah"Torah" + "Ken Ya'aseh" (as if Chukah)YES
Todah — 4 bread types"Torah" (Hukash to Nazir)YES — four types Me'akev each other
Metzora — 4 species"Zos Tihyeh Toras" = emphatic, like ChukahYES
Yom Kippur — 2 goats"Chukah"YES — Me'akev each other
Rav's final rule: "Torah" OR "Chukah" — either word alone suffices. Two words are not required.
