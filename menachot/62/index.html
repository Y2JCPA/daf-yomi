(Continuation of Beraisa): Question: How is Tenufah (of Shelamim) done?
Answer: The Eimurim (Chelev, Yoseres ha'Kaved and kidneys) are put on the hand. The chest and foreleg are put on top of them;
Question: (We do not bring bread with a regular Shelamim.) What does this refer to?
Answer (Rav Papa): It refers to the Milu'im. (Shitah Mekubetzes - he did not say Eil Nazir or Todah, even though those apply for all generations, for we learn those from the Milu'im.)
Question: Why are the chest and foreleg on top of the Eimurim?
