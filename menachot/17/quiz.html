Menachot 17 - Quiz

Test your knowledge of the daf

1. According to the sharp scholars of Pumbedita, burning the Kometz with intent to burn the Levonah tomorrow creates what?

A) Pasul, no Karet — the Kometz is not a Matir for the Levonah
B) Pigul + Karet — intent for the other Matir is equivalent to intent for the entire Matir
C) Kosher — no improper intent regarding the Shirayim
D) Only Pigul according to R. Meir, not Chachamim
The scholars of Pumbedita say: Haktarah IS Mefagel another Haktarah. When one has intent for the Levonah during Haktaras ha'Kometz, it is as if one intended for the ENTIRE permitting factor. Even Chachamim agree in this case — Pigul + Karet.

2. What is Rav Chisda's (citing Rav) ruling on this question?

A) Pigul + Karet — the combined burning creates Pigul
B) NO Pigul — Haktarah does not Mefagel another Haktarah; the Kometz is not a Matir for the Levonah
C) Only R. Meir would say Pigul; Chachamim say Pasul only
D) Only if the same Kohen burns both Kometz and Levonah
Rav Chisda citing Rav: Haktarah does NOT Mefagel another Haktarah. The Kometz-burning permits the Shirayim — not the Levonah. Pigul requires intent regarding what the act actually permits. Since Kometz doesn't permit Levonah, such intent cannot create Pigul.

3. In Rav Hamnuna's case (intent to burn Levonah AND eat Shirayim, both tomorrow), what creates the Pigul?

A) Two weak intents always combine to create a valid intent
B) The combined intent spreads throughout the entire Minchah (Pashta Machashavah b'Chulah Minchah) — encompassing all components
C) The Levonah intent alone is sufficient according to Rav Hamnuna
D) Two Kohanim each contributed one type of intent which merged
Rav Ada bar Ahavah explains: neither intent alone creates Pigul (Haktarah doesn't Mefagel Haktarah; half-Matir alone isn't Pigul). But COMBINED — the intent spreads across the ENTIRE Minchah (Pashta Machashavah b'Chulah Minchah). The whole Minchah is encompassed — this achieves Pigul.

4. Why is R. Yakov bar Idi's proof from the Mishnah (one lamb doesn't Mefagel the other) rejected?

A) Lambs and Levonah are completely different types of Korbanos
B) The two lambs were never placed in one Kli together, while Kometz and Levonah ARE in one Kli — making them potentially as one unit
C) The Mishnah refers only to R. Meir's position
D) Lambs are inner Korbanos; the Minchah is an outer offering
The Gemara rejects the proof: the two Shavuot lambs are never placed together in one Kli. But the Kometz and Levonah of a Minchah ARE in the same Kli together. This Kli-sharing may make them 'as one' — meaning the Kometz COULD be a Matir for the Levonah. The lambs analogy doesn't apply.

5. What is the minimum consensus regarding Haktaras Kometz done with intent to eat the Shirayim tomorrow?

A) Kosher — eating intent during burning has no legal effect
B) Pigul + Karet by universal Tannaic agreement
C) Pasul at minimum — all agree it is at least Pasul; whether Pigul is a machloket
D) Karet applies only if the Levonah was also burned with the same intent
The Gemara corrects a Baraisa-reciter: say 'all agree it is Pasul' — not 'all agree it is Pigul.' Tannaim DO disagree whether this reaches Pigul (R. Meir) or remains merely Pasul. The only consensus is that it is at least Pasul — disqualified.
