Can burning the Kometz with intent to burn the Levonah tomorrow create Pigul? The sharp scholars of Pumbedita say YES; Rav Chisda citing Rav says NO. Rav Hamnuna presents a combined-intent case resolved by the principle "Pashta Machashavah b'Chulah Minchah."

Can Burning Mefagel Burning?

Pumbedita Scholars

  • YES — Haktarah IS Mefagel another Haktarah
  • Intent for the other Matir = as if intended for the entire Matir
  • Even Chachamim agree in this case

Rav Chisda citing Rav

  • NO — Haktarah does NOT Mefagel another Haktarah
  • The Kometz is not a Matir FOR the Levonah
  • Pigul requires intent regarding what the act permits
Key distinction: The Kometz permits the Shirayim — not the Levonah. Intent regarding the Levonah during Kometz-burning cannot create Pigul (Rav). But the Kometz and Levonah ARE in the same Kli — are they "as one"? (Pumbedita's argument)

Rav Hamnuna's Combined Case and Rav Ada's Resolution

Rav Hamnuna: If Haktaras Kometz was done with intent to burn the Levonah tomorrow AND eat the Shirayim tomorrow — the offering is Pigul!
GEM
What is the Chidush? If Haktarah Mefagels Haktarah — just say "intent to burn Levonah." If half-Matir creates Pigul — just say "intent to eat Shirayim." Why state BOTH?
RAA
Rav Ada bar Ahavah: Haktarah does NOT Mefagel Haktarah AND half-Matir alone is not Pigul. But COMBINED — the intent SPREADS throughout the entire Minchah (Pashta Machashavah b'Chulah Minchah). Two insufficient intents together achieve Pigul.
